Selena Gomez showing 'beautiful personality' has Hailey Bieber's 'seal of approval'

July 29, 2024
blog

Selena Gomez is enjoying quality time with her younger sister, Gracie, who she refers to as her “little-me.” The 10-year-old is the daughter of Selena’s mom, Mandy Teefey, and stepfather Brian Teefey. A couple of years ago, Gracie made her red carpet debut when her older sister Selena brought her to the Los Angeles premiere of Frozen 2.

She’s been making regular appearances on Selena Gomez‘s highly followed Instagram page, and she’s now stated that her younger sister is ‘cooler than she’ll ever be.’ Fans compliment Selena’s ‘beautiful personality’ as she shows her sibling relationship on Insta, and it looks like Hailey Bieber has given it a silent seal of approval.

Selena Gomez posts with ‘mini-me’ Gracie

Taking to her 426 million Instagram followers, Selena shared a mirror selfie with her younger sister, which she captioned: “I love my little me -she’s cooler than I’ll ever be.”

The two can be seen posing for the ‘gram as the Wizards of Waverly Place TV alum shows off her fresh manicure, paired with gold rings. The sisters are ‘twinning’ in matching black outfits, as Selena accessorized hers with gold hoop earrings.

The 30-year-old is extremely close to her half-sister, Gracie Elliott Teefey, despite the more than 20-year age difference.

The two are regularly spotted together on social media, just recently the Lose You to Love Me singer posted a snap of the two in New York which she captioned: “Missing NY and my sissy.”

Fans praise the star’s ‘beautiful personality’

Of course, fans rushed to the comment of the sister selfie to comment on the cuteness of the sibling’s relationship, with some praising Selena’s ‘beautiful personality.’ It seems like Hailey Bieber also loved the cuteness of the pic, as she gave it a like on the ‘gram.

“Imagine having SELENA GOMEZ as your sister,” penned one.

Another fan wrote: “This why I love Selena because she didn’t let Hollywood change her beautiful personality.”

“Please adopt me,” begged another.

“Oh, she’s definitely got that Alex Russo element in her,” one wrote.

Selena Gomez’s sister ‘supports’ Taylor Swift

Earlier this week, Grace dyed her hair purple for Selena’s bestie, Taylor Swift’s Speak Now album, as shown in an Instagram video.

Gomez then asked her younger sis if she’d ever dye her hair for her album, to which she responded: “Sure, I will,” before the siblings erupted into laughter.

The 30-year-old captioned the video: “When your friends are cooler than you.”
