Kaia Gerber and Cindy Crawfords chicest-ever style moments: from the mother-daughter duos icon

September 15, 2024
blog

In 2018, the Omega brand ambassadors appeared in a Mother’s Day campaign for the luxury watch manufacturer. Both sported casual white ensembles, soft beachy waves and coordinating timepieces from the luxury brand’s Trésor collection.

The stylish mother-daughter duo continued to sport similar looks during a fashion week trip last year in March. On the streets of Paris, both donned matching off-duty separates consisting of camel-coloured wool coats, black combat boots and sunglasses. However, that wasn’t the only time we’ve had to do a double take when the pair were seen together.

Here are some of Cindy Crawford and Kaia Gerber’s most memorable style moments thus far.

1. Edward Enninful OBE A Visible Man, September 2022

At the book launch for Edward Enninful’s A Visible Man, the mother-daughter duo wore skin-baring ensembles in earthy colours.

Gerber opted for a cut-out mini dress from Nensi Dojaka while Crawford, 57, chose an equally fashionable halter-neck top and pleated leather skirt.

2. Omega San Francisco Grand Opening, November 2021

Both stunned in elegant evening gowns and matching timepieces on the red carpet of the Omega San Francisco Grand Opening VIP celebration at De Young Museum in California.

3. Annual InStyle Awards, November 2021

The glamorous duo stood out in dazzling sequins and embellishments at the sixth annual InStyle Awards in 2021.

Crawford sported beige heels and a striking metallic sleeveless gown by Missoni. Her lookalike daughter, meanwhile, wore an Alexander McQueen tank dress featuring ombré crystals and a cascading fringe skirt.

4. Birthday bustier

In 2019, Gerber celebrated her 18th birthday in a custom Atelier Versace leather bustier and an embellished mini skirt.

The head-turning bondage look was a modern rendition of the one her supermodel mother famously wore to the MTV Awards back in 1992.

5. A Sense of Home Annual Gala, November 2019

They looked more like sisters when they donned similar make-up styles and sophisticated all-black looks at A Sense of Home’s first-ever annual gala – The Backyard Bowl in the latter half of 2019.

6. British Fashion Awards, December 2018

Fashion royalty: Salma Hayek and stepdaughter Mathilde Pinault

For the occasion, Gerber slipped into an asymmetrical cut-out top with flared pants and black heels. Her mother, on the other hand, looked the epitome of elegance in a velvet off-the shoulder gown with a thigh-high slit. That same evening, Gerber won the award for model of the year.
