20 Blunt Cuts for Every Face Shape and Hair Texture

July 08, 2024
blog

ICYMI: Blunt haircuts are back — and better than ever. While the traditional blunt cut refers to a style with one uniform length, today's interpretation is, at times, more open-ended. For instance, modern variations may feature subtle layers with blunt ends. Although this breaks from the cut's strict no-layers rule, it still maintains its clean, polished aesthetic. So no matter your hair length, texture, or even face shape, you're bound to find a blunt cut that works for you. To inspire your next salon appointment, we're sharing our roundup of 25 blunt hairstyles as modeled by our favorite A-listers.

From textured crops to stick-straight lobs, keep scrolling for the best celeb-inspired blunt cuts.

Lana Condor

Beachy waves and strategic layers add a bit of texture and movement to Lana Condor's center-parted bob with blunt ends.

Jenna Dewan

Jenna Dewan keeps her look smooth and sleek by tucking one side of her hair behind her ear.

Dua Lipa

Pair your sleek blunt bob with '60s lashes and shimmery shadow like Dua Lipa.

Hailey Bieber

Hailey Bieber adds loose waves to her bob with razor-sharp ends. Notice it appears slightly longer in the back, too.

Jourdan Dunn

Use a flat iron (and a heat protectant spray) to copy the undone bends in Jourdan Dunn's asymmetrical bob.

Emma Roberts

Emma Roberts goes blunt all over with a sharp-edged lob and micro bangs.

Irina Shayk

The model wears her bob sleek and parted down the middle, giving it a classic vibe. The hairstyle also draws attention to the slight angle of the haircut.

Gigi Hadid

Gigi Hadid's brushed-back bob is one of her most memorable red carpet moments.

Viola Davis

Viola Davis styles her blunt, long bob straight and with a side part.

Lucy Boynton

Lucy Boynton's bob looks will go down in red carpet history. For the 76th Annual Golden Globes, the actress showed off a voluminous and super wavy version of her go-to cut.

Charlize Theron

A deep side-part and barely-there waves switch up the vibe of Charlize Theron's blunt bob.

Jessica Chastain

Jessica Chastain wears her blunt bob with barrel waves on one side and tucked behind the ear on the other. The final look is glam all over.

Cardi B

An ultra-precise center part adds drama to Cardi B's already head-turning platinum bob.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift pairs her chin-length bob with equally blunt bangs.

Kat Graham

Soft yet blunt fringe add a special element to Kat Graham's blunt bob.

Kelly Rowland

Kelly Rowland styled her blunt bob with a center part, one of this year's biggest hair trends.

Margot Robbie

The actress wears her blunt style with a side part and slightly flipped ends, giving it a vintage spin.

Rihanna

Parted down the middle, Rihanna's chin-length blunt bob is a must-see.

Yara Shahidi

Yara Shahidi's chin-length bob with bangs is a dream. For curly hair, stylists will incorporate barely-there layers to achieve the desired — and seemingly blunt — shape.

Lucy Hale

Dark roots lend contrast to Lucy Hale's dirty blonde bob.
