Miranda Lambert Stand Up for Lainey Wilson with Epic Comment

March 03, 2024
blog

If we ever need a country music star to come to our defense, let it be Miranda Lambert. The "Mama's Broken Heart" singer dropped an epic comment on Lainey Wilson's Instagram post, and we won't be recovering any time soon.

Yellowstone star Lainey Wilson is currently on tour with Luke Combs, and the two made a stop in Vancouver. Lainey shared a series of photos from the event, including one that got a big reaction from her fans and celebrity friends.

Lainey's post includes photos of her rocking out onstage in very colorful striped bellbottoms, a pink button-down shirt, and casual straw cowboy hat. In one photo, she has a bewildered look on her face and holds up a white T-shirt with the phrase, "Lainey Wilson: Best Ass in Country Music."

In case you missed it, Lainey went viral earlier this year after a concert video showcased her backside, starting the Lainey Wilson dump truck trend. Lainey has maintained a good sense of humor about the whole situation, telling her fans "However you found me, I’m happy. I’m happy you’re here" and even creating a funny dump truck meme.

Lainey's boyfriend Devlin Hodges hopped in the comments section to leave the flirty message, "I’d agreeeeee," but fellow country star Miranda took the opportunity to correct the T-shirt's message. She wrote, "Best Ass kicker in country music is what they meant to say." Other fans jumped in with messages like, "Gonna tell my kids this is the queen" and "Keep killin it miss ma’am."

Oh, and if you're looking for Lainey's striped bellbottoms, they're the Laser Striped Wanderer Flare Jean from Wrangler. The iconic denim company recently announced that Lainey is the new face of the brand.
