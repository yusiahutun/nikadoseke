Revered for his over-the-top lyrics and equally quirky delivery, Ugly God is an American Hip-Hop artist best remembered for his 2016 viral release, Water.

A key proponent of the so-called Soundcloud Era, the rapper - born Royce Davison - was one of the ten musicians who made up the XXL Freshmen Class in 2017.

In less than a decade-long musical journey, Ugly God already packs an impressive catalog. Wiz Khalifa, Lil Yatchy and Famous Dex are some of his more renowned collaborators.

Bio - Height, Parents & Age

Ugly God was born to a Dominican father and African-American mother in 1996. A native of Indiana, he grew up in parts of the Mid-West as well as the South.

By the time the "RARI" hitmaker touched his teenage years, his biological parents had already parted ways. Despite everything, he remained connected with his father's Dominican roots and even learned the language.
