Meghan Markle's favourite scented candle brands: Soho House, Diptyque &amp; more

Meghan Markle is a big fan of a scented candle...

    In her recent tell-all interview with , Meghan Markle shared a rare insight into her family life and home. Since closing her lifestyle blog back in 2017, the Duchess reveals little about her latest buys, but we now know her home fragrance of choice is the Bassett Rose Water Candle from Soho House.

    Meghan shares her £10 million home in Montecito, California with her husband Prince Harry and their two children, Archie and Lilibet.

    "Meghan, relaxing in a cosy chair, gazes over all that is climate-controlled and high-ceilinged and sun-dappled and perfectly marshmallowy, and hers," wrote Allison P. Davis for . "An invisible hand has lit a Soho House–branded rose-water candle (the founder, Nick Jones, is a friend from ‘long before I met Harry,’ she says), and that scent fills the air."

    Bassett Rose Water Candle, from £40, Soho House

    Said to be an ‘ode to the English rose’, it’s inspired by the walled garden at Babington House and includes notes of rose, geranium, lemon and bergamot, topped off with guaiac wood, musk, ginger and spice.

    If you don't want to wait, The White Company has a similar and more affordable fragrance. Flowers is light and romantic with notes of roses, jasmine and neroli.
