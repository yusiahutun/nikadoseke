The Duke and Duchess of Sussex's Oprah interview featured the most gorgeous chairs

    Almost 50million people around the world tuned into Prince Harry and Meghan Markle's tell-all interview with Oprah Winfrey. But besides the bombshells dropped by the Duke and Duchess of Sussex, viewers were also paying attention to the garden chairs the trio sat on.

    The original acacia furniture, the 'Burchett' Outdoor Club Chairs set, is designed by Christopher Knight Home – the eponymous home furnishings brand founded by actor Christopher Knight, who played Peter Brady on the 1960s US TV classic.
