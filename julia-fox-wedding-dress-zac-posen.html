Julia Fox Is an Ethereal Bride in an Off-the-Shoulder Wedding Dress

February 15, 2024
blog

Julia Fox has quickly risen through the ranks to be recognized as one of Hollywood's most avant-garde style stars—and for good reason. Whether strutting in a pair of dangerously low-rise leather trousers or grocery shopping in just her bra and underwear, the Uncut Gems actress knows how to turn heads.

Her latest look is no exception. Fox made for an ethereal bride while wearing a couture wedding gown designed by Zac Posen, as seen in photos shared to her and Posen's Instagram Stories.

The showstopping, off-the-shoulder dress included pleated detailing on the bodice and a sweeping train. Though she forwent her signature smeared black eye shadow, Fox still took an innovative route with her glam, opting for a sculptural updo with sci-fi undertones.

Instagram

Instagram

Rather than walking down the aisle, she wore the couture bridal look to promote her forthcoming movie, The Trainer, the Tony Kaye-directed dark comedy in which she stars opposite Vito Schnabel and Steven Van Zandt.

Instagram

Schnabel, an internationally recognized art gallerist, co-wrote the script for the movie with Jeff Solomon.

"The idea of the film came into my head about ten years ago and it’s incredible to have it come together with Tony Kaye, whom I’ve spent many months working on this project with as our director and cinematographer," Schnabel said, per Deadline. "I see him as one of the most talented artists in sound manufacturing and motion pictures and it’s an honor to be able to work with him."
