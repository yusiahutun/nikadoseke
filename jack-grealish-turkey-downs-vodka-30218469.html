'Turkey' Jack Grealish has bottle of vodka poured down throat in boozy Man City parade

blog

Jack Grealish called himself a turkey and downed vodka during Manchester City's title parade - after admitting that he hadn't been to sleep.

City players took to a purpose built stage in Manchester after a bus tour of the city having added Champions League glory to their Premier League and FA Cup crowns.

Grealish, 27, looked worse for wear in a high vis jacket after 24 hours of partying including a 12-hour stop over in Ibiza.

He said on the microphone: "Guys, guys. Seriously, hold on one second."

When asked if he was okay by presenter Natalie Pike, Grealish added: "Yeah... just basically, for the past 24 hours I've had the best day and night. To be fair, I don't think I've slept."

Natalie then asked for the microphone back but he refused, saying: "Na, na, one sec. Right I'm a turkey and the turkey needs feeding."

Kyle Walker then said: "Jack, Jack? Is the pigeon thirsty?"

Grealish then stood bolt upright and threw his head back as Kalvin Phillips poured what looked to be Grey Goose vodka down his throat to the delight of fans and colleagues.

The players didn't stay on stage for long before heading off to their party, with fans left drenched by the wet weather as rain threatened to spoil the day.

The parade was delayed by 30 minutes to start with, as the club live stream failed to show the players on the bus due to technical difficulties.

The presenters did catch up with Pep Guardiola and Ilkay Gundogan as the parade came to an end on the corner of Princess Street and Portland Street.

The captain said with a short message: "Thank you very much for all the support all season it's been incredible and we've got three trophies!"

His manager had told fans: "What a parade! We had to be the best parade with this rain or it is not Manchester! We don't want sunshine! We want rain! The perfect one!"
