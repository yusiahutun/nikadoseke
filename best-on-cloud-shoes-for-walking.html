What to look for in On’s Cloud shoes

Harper's Bazaar

When you’re choosing a walking or running sneaker, it’s best to try on a few styles to find a fit and heel drop that best suits your body and gait. A chunky-soled shoe will be more cushioned, offering a bit more support. A lightweight shoe is more suited to speed.
