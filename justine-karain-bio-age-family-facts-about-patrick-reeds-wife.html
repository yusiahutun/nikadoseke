Justine Karain – Bio (Age)

After graduating from high school, Justine Karain then attended Louisiana State University for Health Service Administration for her undergraduate education. Asides being an A-student, Karain was also an excellent swimmer and played soccer during her high school days.

Career

Justine Karain’s career began as a health worker, specifically as a caregiver, otherwise known as a caddie. As a requirement for full ‘caddie-ship,’ she worked full-time as a nurse in training. She was, however, replaced by her brother Kessler when she became pregnant with their first child. She soon switched roles from health worker to her husband’s biggest full-time supporter on and off course. However, as motherhood responsibilities slowly crept in, she was no longer readily available on the golf course to cheer her husband up. She focused on raising their two kids, instead.

Justine Karain and Patrick Reed have a close-knit family. So far their marriage has produced two children, a daughter they christened Windsor-Wells (b. May 2014) and a son, Barrett Benjamin, born on December 6, 2017.
