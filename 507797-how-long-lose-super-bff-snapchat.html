The Super BFF emoji is two hearts that appears beside your friend's name. It shows how close you are to them. Snapchat adds this emoji when someone has been your best friend on the app for two months at a stretch.

What happens on Snapchat after Super BFF? You'll get a gold heart after the two hearts emoji for Super BFF. The gold heart emoji shows you've sent and received most snaps from this person.
