A hopeless mother is going through an excruciating moment after the death of her little children.

The mum lost five of her six children to a house fire that snatched their innocent lives on New Year's eve.

Kenise Robinson lost four boys and one girl in the fire incident that took place on Dartmouth Avenue, Buffalo, New York City, in the US.

A lot to process

The adorable kids were in their grandparents' home, Clarence and Lisa Liggans when the fire broke out and ripped them as noted by WIVB4.
