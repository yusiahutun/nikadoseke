Who is Kacie McDonnell?

Kacie McDonnell is an American reporter who is famous for being an anchor is NESN in Boston, Massachusetts.

She is also eminent for her work as a Traffic Reporter and a Morning Anchor on FOX Television along with her position as ‘On Air Model’ for ‘QVC’.

Age, Nationality, Parents and Education

Kacie McDonnell was born on July 30, 1990, in Pottsville, Pennsylvania, United States of America. McDonnell belongs to a Caucasian ethnic background.

She was born to Michele McDonnell and Ken McDonnell. She was raised by her parents along with her two siblings Isabelle McDonnell and Lochlan McDonnell.

From a very early age in life, Kacie McDonnell had a sheer interest in making her career as a reporter in a reputed Communication company.

Education

For the sake of her fostering interest in reporting and journalism, she has studied bachelor’s in Communications. Firstly, she attended Nativity BVM High School in 2004.

After Kacie McDonnell graduated from Nativity BVM High School in 2010, she applied and joined Villanova University and majored in Communications. This was her first step to being a successful reporter in the coming future.

Kacie McDonnell: Professional Life, Career

Kacie McDonnell initiated her career after she graduated from Villanova University in 2012. She worked as an ‘On Air Model’ at QVC for three and a half years and moved to ‘FOX Television’ as an Eagles Pregame and Sideline Reporter.

After 5 months of her working as a Sideline Reporter, she was promoted to Traffic Reporter and as a Morning anchor on ‘FOX Television’.

In August 2014, Kacie McDonnell left ‘FOX Television’ and joined ‘Turner sports’ whereby, she was a Courtside Reporter in 2016 ‘NCAA Men’s Final Tour’ which was followed by her work as a reporter for ‘KSHB’, The EW Scripps Company.

Before working as an Anchor in NESN, Boston, she was appointed as a Postgame Host for ‘Sporting Kansas City’. Currently, she is working for NESN.
