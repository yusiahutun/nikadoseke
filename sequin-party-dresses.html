The 10 best sequin dresses to buy for Christmas party season

Taller Marmo

Taller Marmo

Now 40% Off

Prepare to make an entrance this festive season in Taller Marmo's burnt orange sequin mini dress. Featuring the label's classic one-shouldered elegant sleeve and feathered trim, you'll be the best-dressed guest at every party.

Rotate

Scandi label Rotate's beautiful sequin gown is perfect for those more dressy events on your calendar, and the muted colour means it will never go out of style.

Self-Portrait

Self-Portrait

Opting for rental where you can is not only a great way to avoid buying something you only wear once, but is also a great way to try out brands and different shapes ahead of making a wardrobe investment. Turn to rental service Hurr to dance the night away in this delightfully shimmery lilac Self-Portrait number.

Paco Rabanne

Paco Rabanne

A chain-disk dress from Paco Rabanne is an investment worth making with the design remaining a fashion editor-favourite for decades. Team with sky-high platform heels and a matching shimmering eyeshadow for the ultimate Christmas party outfit.

Rixo

Rixo's halterneck sequin gown is all about ladylike elegance. We love its fun stripe and ribbon tie-up back; style yours with a pair of strappy sandals, a bold red lip and shimmering statement earrings, and you're good to go.

Ganni

Does anything say Christmas more than red sequins? Ganni has the perfect party dress – its maxi take on festive dressing is delightfully elegant, team with dazzling earrings for that extra touch.

16Arlington

16Arlington

Not only have rich chocolatey and latte hues been all over the autumn/winter 2023 catwalks, but the colour is also set to become central to your festive wardrobe thanks to 16Arlington. This maxi dress offers up just the right amount of sexiness, via its cut-out neckline and subtle leg split.

Stine Goya

Stine Goya

Now 35% Off

Stine Goya's take on the sequin party dress is a win-win; not only is this dress sleek and sophisticated, but it's also extremely comfortable. The Scandi designer never compromises on good style and comfort, making this dress a smart investment this festive season.

Philosophy Di Lorenzo Serafini

Philosophy Di Lorenzo Serafini

Now 40% Off

While we are all in favour of a midi or mini length, a maxi probably has the biggest impact. Philosophy Di Lorenzo Serafini's floor-sweeping gown is ideal for formal affairs, while its open back and padded shoulders give the design a fun twist.

Annie's Ibiza

Annie's Ibiza

You can't get much more festive than this beautiful silver design by Annie's Ibiza, which features hundreds of beautiful sequins and also a dancing mermaid motif. This 'La Sirena' dress has the most flattering fit – cinched in at the waist with an elegant high neckline.
