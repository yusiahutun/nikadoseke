From A Businessman To Pastor

Barry started his carpet cleaning business called "ZZZZ Best" at the early age of 15 in his parent’s garage. His company made an escalating growth to 1400 employees in just four years. His company became a national sensation and was valued to be around $280 million at that time. After three years, ZZZZ Best gave authority to private shareholders and the company’s stock worth $18 per share in 1986. During the prime time of his company, he also had Genovese mafia among his clientele.

The company also had a T-shirt printed ZZZZ BEST CARPET CLEANING & FURNITURE sold on a global online platform, Redbubble, for $19.90 per piece.
