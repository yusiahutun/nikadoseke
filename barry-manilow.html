Who is Barry Manilow?

Barry Manilow is a well-recognised American Singer, songwriter, producer and TV personality who has made a huge name in the American musical industry and had a long span of career of more than about 7 decades. He has given 13 platinum and 6 multi-platinum albums that all got hit and praised by the audiences. Barry Manilow was born on 17th June 1943, in Brooklyn, New York to his parents Edna Manilow and Harold Kelliher.

Barry Manilow is an award-winning singer who started his career in 1964 and has been gaining a huge number of popularity till now. He has recorded 51 top 40 singles on the Adult Contemporary Chart out of which 13 hit at number One, 28 appeared on the top ten and the rest 36 appeared on the top 20. The most hit songs of Barry Manilow include Mandy, Could It Be Magic, Looks Like We Made It, Can’t Smile Without You, I Write The Songs, Copacabana, Weekend In New England and many more.
