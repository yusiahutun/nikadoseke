Travis Kelce apparently made the first move

When Kelce attended Swift’s Eras Tour in Kansas City, Missouri, in July, he said he tried to give the “Anti-Hero” singer his number, per Men’s Health.

He’s an NFL superstar

Swift may be a musical superstar but Kelce is celeb royalty too, since he’s a famous NFL player. At 33, the same age as Swift, the Ohio-born athlete is an American football tight end for the Kansas City Chiefs.

He was in his own reality TV dating show

Beyond American football, Kelce has dabbled in showbiz as well. The two-time Super Bowl champion starred in his own reality TV dating show Catching Kelce in 2016. Or, as StyleCaster puts it, he once “dated 50 women at the same time” to find “the one”.

He ended up pursuing a relationship with contestant Maya Benberry, but it didn’t last long, per Today.

He once hosted Saturday Night Live

In March, Kelce received praise for his hilarious sketches on SNL. The athlete managed to garner plenty of laughs from the audience, showcasing his witty sense of humour and easy-going off-field personality – qualities that we’re confident Swift would also appreciate.

He hosts a podcast with his brother

Both American football stars, Travis and his brother Jason also host their own podcast, New Heights, to “provide next-level access to life in the league as it unfolds”, according to the show’s description on Spotify. The bio adds that they are “[American] football’s funniest family duo”.
