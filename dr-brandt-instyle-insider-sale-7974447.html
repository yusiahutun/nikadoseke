Dr. Brandt Age-Defying Exfoliator

If you’ve ever experienced a professional microdermabrasion treatment, you’ll be well aware of how skin appears significantly smoother immediately afterwards thanks to its pore-minimizing effects. I have many at-home microdermabrasion tools that deliver similarly spectacular results, but on days I can’t be bothered to power everything on and fumble with buttons, I turn to this zesty, enlivening scrub (which is, TBH, almost as powerful). Unlike your average physical face scrub, this exfoliator contains ultra-fine, evenly sized microgranules, which mimic the feel and effect of an in-office microdermabrasion treatment. Use once or twice a week, and I can almost guarantee you’ll be wowed by an unprecedented glow. 

Dr. Brandt Icy Gel Moisturizer

Frankly, this is what I’ve dubbed my “hangover moisturizer,” or my “one too many slices of salty pizza” moisturizer. In other words, it’s an excellent queller of all genres of puffiness thanks to its cooling feel and instantly calming effect. Truthfully, I’ve yet to experience a product with similar icy-cooling power, and I use this stuff down to the very last squeeze. Plus, the cooling power delivers a side of sensorial bliss and — on mornings after I’ve had one too many glasses of Chardonnay — a wash of soothing relief. 

Dr. Brandt Firming Day Cream

I’ve only just started experimenting with this youth-preserving moisturizer, but I already know I’m in love. The formula is the perfect weight — not greasy, yet not overly light so as to feel it’s doing nothing. My skin looks immediately plump, dewy, and bouncy, with an all-over glassy glow. Over time, I’m expecting a firmer-looking face; the formula does, after all, contain collagen-boosting, plant-derived stem cells in addition to peptides — a dermatologist-loved, face-firming protein. Don’t miss this moisturizer’s neck cream counterpart, which is designed to lift and tighten lax skin in the notoriously tricky-to-treat region. 

Dr. Brandt No More Baggage Eye De-Puffing Gel

A best-seller for Dr. Brandt, this skin-tightening eye cream — which the brand dubs its “most viral” product — tightens lax under eye skin in five minutes, smoothing the appearance of fine lines and deflating the look of bags. Peptides work over time to restore lost elasticity, while caffeine depuffs on contact.
