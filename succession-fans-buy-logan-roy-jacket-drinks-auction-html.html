Live like the Roys! Succession fans can buy Logan Roy's jacket and drinks set in new auction of cost

June 05, 2024
blog

Fans of the TV drama Succession will be able to buy costumes and props from the hit show in an auction.

There are 236 props and costumes up for sale from the HBO show, which has run for four series since 2017.

The sale takes place on January 13 at Heritage Auctions in Dallas, US.

Although there are no pre-sale estimates for the individual lots, some of the items, especially the costumes, are expected to sell for thousands. Interest in them is said to be 'extremely strong', Heritage Auctions said.

Actor Brian Cox who plays Logan Roy on the hit HBO drama series

Logan Roy's jacket (pictured) as seen in Season 1, Episode 1 of the show is available to buy

A spokesperson for Heritage said: 'Browsing this auction is akin to stepping into the Roys' endless closets full of fashion that merely whisper wealth.

'It's brand-name bingo, four seasons worth of coveted, crave-able clothing available in a single sitting.

'This is a singular opportunity for fans to own something iconic from one of their favourite characters or sets.

'Who wouldn't want to own the ludicrously capricious bag or a pair of Kendall or Greg's sunglasses?

'Truly, there is something for everyone who loved this show, which is anyone who watched a second of Succession.'

Also available is a drinks set (pictured) and designer outfits worn by his bickering children Kendall, Roman and Shiv

Logan Roy seen drinking from a glass tumbler on Succession. Many of the items are expected to sell for thousands

Some of the items available to purchase include media mogul Logan Roy's jacket, a drinks set and designer outfits worn by his bickering children Kendall, Roman and Shiv.

There are nine stylish power suits worn by actress Sarah Snook during key scenes involving her character Shiv in the critically acclaimed show.

These include a black cashmere jacket, trousers and scarf ensemble worn by Shiv at her father's funeral in season four.

There is also a Tom Ford black blazer and Sergio Hudson tuxedo trousers she wore at elder brother Connor's wedding, also in season four.

You can also pick up a Max Mara size 6, black and white dress that Shiv wore in season two where she deals with a company sex scandal.

There is a Lanvin charcoal grey designer suit worn by Kendall in season one as well as a pair of his Oliver Peoples sunglasses and a pair of his size 10 Lanvin high-top trainers.

There are nine stylish power suits worn by actress Sarah Snook during key scenes involving her character Shiv in the critically acclaimed show. Pictured: Shiv, played by Sarah Snook, with her  Succession family

Other items include a copy of a press statement read out by Shiv after Logan's sudden death. Pictured, Succession's Shiv Roy dresses for business 

Greg Hirsch's Doderick the Dog costume and customised backpack that he wore in the very first episode are also up for grabs.

There is a large Burberry handbag which was carried by Greg's date, Bridget, for Logan Roy's birthday party in season four.

Also, you can get your hands on mocked-up magazine covers profiling the feuding family's troubles

One of these is for Forbes magazine and has a photo of Kendall Roy.

Other props include the Waystar Royco lanyard ID passes from Kendall and for Tom Wambsgans, who was played by British actor Matthew Macfadyen.

Other items include a copy of a press statement read out by Shiv after Logan's sudden death in season four and five copies of the order of service for his funeral.
