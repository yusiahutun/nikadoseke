Holly Willoughby goes from yummy to slummy as she lights up at V Festival

By Chris Johnson for MailOnline
Updated: 10:32 EDT, 24 August 2009

She's usually picture of fresh-faced sophistication, but it was a very different story yesterday as Holly Willoughby slummed it at V Festival yesterday.

The Xtra Factor host, who was recently named as Fern Britton's replacement on This Morning, was caught walking around the festival grounds with a cigarette hanging out of her mouth.

It is believed to be the first time the 28-year-old has been photographed smoking and goes in the face of her clean-living public image.

The Xtra Factor presenter was spotted stalking around Weston Park in Staffordshire with a drink in her hand, one of two of the venues for the event.

Holly, who gave birth to first child Harry in May, dressed down in a floral skirt for the occasion, which she wore over leggings.

It comes in stark contrast to the polished image that has endeared her to so many and helped her secure her spot on the sofa.

Holly this week opened up about landing the job on This Morning, telling Hello! magazine: 'It has been the most incredible year.

'When I look back I think, "Gosh, I’ve become a mum and have my dream job in the space of two months." It’s all a bit mad. I have to pinch myself a bit.

Casual: She dressed down in a floral skirt which she wore over leggings

'I’m a massive fan of the show. I’ve always watched it and loved it. It’s the kind of presenting I love; chatting and interviewing on a live, current show that’s reactive to what’s going on. You can’t beat live TV.'

Holly will be on the programme from next month when it returns after a summer break.

She was selected as the new host alongside Philip Schofield after Fern Britton quit after 10-years.

It was a coup for the for the young presenter, who is also the co-host of Dancing On Ice with Schofield.

The new mother will have a lot on her plate, juggling motherhood along with the demanding This Morning schedule on top of her Xtra Factor duties for ITV2.

Let's hope the stress of her new hectic life doesn't drive her to the cigarette too often.

However, it looks like Holly's debut may be delayed because she will be required overseas at the beginning of September of Xtra Factor filming.

The four mentors usually take their final six acts to a different country, such as Simon Cowell's LA home, to whittle the list down to three each.
