She shed 80lbs by going on a postpartum Keto diet.

And Jenna Jameson shared some of the other benefits of the diet with her roughly 353,000 Instagram followers this Wednesday.

'Not only has my face changed from weightloss, but eating whole foods and next to zero processed foods has made my skin better,' the 44-year-old ex-porn star wrote.
