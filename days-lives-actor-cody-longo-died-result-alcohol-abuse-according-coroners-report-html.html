Days of Our Lives actor Cody Longo died as a result of alcohol abuse according to coroner's report

August 11, 2024
blog

Cody Longo's official cause of death has been revealed.

An autopsy on the Days of Our Lives actor revealed he died as a result of alcohol abuse, according to documents obtained by TMZ.

The report said the 34-year-old's death was caused by 'chronic ethanol abuse.'

The postmortem also indicated the body had already begun to decompose by the time it was found and the room where he was found was littered with empty alcohol bottles.

Police in Austin, Texas discovered Longo's body on February 8, while conducting a welfare check after receiving a request from his wife Stephanie.

On Father's Day, Longo's widow paid tribute to him in her Instagram stories, sharing photos of him with their three children, Lyla, seven, Elijah, five, and Noah, two.

'Celebrating Cody in heaven. Thankful for the beautiful moments we created and the beautiful babies we created,' she wrote.     

The actor is known for briefly playing the character Nicholas Alamain on the popular soap in 2011.

At the time of his death, a family member told TMZ that the actor had struggled with alcohol abuse for many years and had recently gone to rehab.

His wife reportedly told the site: 'He was an amazing father and the best husband. Our whole world is shattered.'

Longo, who was born in Littleton, Colorado on March 4, 1988, began his career in the entertainment industry by appearing in several music videos before he made his film debut in the 2008 feature Ball Don't Lie.

He subsequently appeared on the small screen for the first time during an episode of Medium that aired the following year.

The late performer went on to appear in supporting roles in various features over the following years, including Fame and Bring It On: Fight to the Finish.
