42 Fathers Day Quotes for Friends

March 21, 2024
blog

Every year, when the third Sunday in June rolls around, our thoughts turn to the central father figures in our lives—our dads, stepdads, grandads, and fathers of our children. From shopping for Father’s Day gifts to what to write in a Father’s Day card to planning special activities, there’s a lot do to. Still, we like to acknowledge all the awesome dads we know. Enter this collection of Father’s Day quotes for friends.

What do you say to a friend on Father's Day? Depending on your friendship, you might choose something inspirational, sentimental, or funny. (If your best bud is the king of dad jokes, you might also peruse our roundup of Father’s Day puns for ideas.) But whatever tone you want to strike, consider these quotes your go-to source for Happy Father’s Day wishes for friends. We even have a section for new dads!

It only takes a moment to send a quick text—or maybe you want to go all out and gather your dad friends for a celebratory Father’s Day brunch. Either way, these quotes are sure to make 'em smile.

Inspirational Father’s Day Quotes for Friends

Jaouad k//Getty Images

“Fathering is not something perfect men do, but something that perfects the man.” —Frank Pittman

“A good father is one of the most unsung, unpraised, unnoticed, and yet one of the most valuable assets in our society.” —Billy Graham

“What makes you a man is not the ability to have a child—it’s the courage to raise one.” ―Barack Obama

“A father is a man who expects his son to be as good a man as he meant to be.” —Frank A. Clark

“A father is neither an anchor to hold us back nor a sail to take us there, but a guiding light whose love shows us the way.” —Unknown

“By the time a man realizes that maybe his father was right, he usually has a son who thinks he’s wrong.” —Charles Wadsworth

“Every father should remember one day his son will follow his example, not his advice.” —Charles Kettering

“A girl's father is the first man in her life, and probably the most influential.” —David Jeremiah

“A father is someone you look up to no matter how tall you grow.” —Unknown

“The quality of a father can be seen in the goals, dreams, and aspirations he sets not only for himself, but for his family.” —Reed Markham

“No man stands taller than when he stoops to help a child.”—Unknown

“Anyone who does anything to help a child in his life is a hero.” —Fred Rogers

Sentimental Father’s Day Quotes for Friends

“Dads are most ordinary men turned by love into heroes, adventurers, story-tellers, and singers of song.” —Pam Brown

“Anyone can be a father, but it takes someone special to be a dad.” —Wade Boggs
