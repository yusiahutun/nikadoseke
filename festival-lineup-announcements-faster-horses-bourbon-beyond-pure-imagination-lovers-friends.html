MI/KY/AZ/NV (CelebrityAccess) – Numerous festivals have announced their artist lineups for the 2024 editions. Please see below for full lineup information.

BROOKLYN, MI – Faster Horses – Festival organizer Live Nation (LN) has announced the Faster Horses Festival is set to return to the Michigan International Speedway for its 11th year in July, featuring a star-studded lineup for 2024. The festival, taking place from July 19 – 21 in Brooklyn, MI, will have first-time headliners Jelly Roll, Lainey Wilson, and Hardy.

The three-day, two-stage festival’s main stage will also host performances by Cody Johnson, Riley Green, Old Dominion, ERNEST, Tyler Hubbard, Sawyer Brown, Tigerlily Gold, Huntergirl, and more. Tickets for the three-day festival go on sale Friday (February 9) at 10 am via the festival’s official website.

LOUISVILLE, KY – Bourbon & Beyond – Festival producers Danny Wimmer Presents (DWP) continue to roll out their 2024 festival lineups and have announced an all-star lineup of music artists for Bourbon & Beyond—the World’s Largest Bourbon & Music Festival – held September 19 – 22, at the Highland Festival Grounds and Kentucky Exposition Center in Louisville. Topping this year’s bill are Dave Matthews Band, Tyler Childers, Neil Young, and Zach Bryan.

Also set to take the stage during the early fall event are Beck, The National, My Morning Jacket, The War on Drugs, Tedeschi Trucks Band, Black Pumas, The Beach Boys, Mt. Joy, Fleet Foxes, Maren Morris, The Head and the Heart, Melissa Etheridge, Lyle Lovett, Sierra Ferrell, Cory Wong, Bruce Hornsby & The Noisemakers, JJ Grey & Mofro, Molly Tuttle & Golden Highway, The War & Treaty, Samantha Fish, Yonder Mountain String Band, Andy Frasco & The U.N and other artists that hail from different corners of the music industry.

Kentucky Governor Andy Beshear, via press release, had this to say about the festival: “The Commonwealth of Kentucky is honored to be hosting Bourbon & Beyond in Louisville this September,” he said. “The festival brings in fans from all over the world and showcases the best of Kentucky, highlighting our rich culture of bourbon, the best in local culinary, and a top-tier musical lineup. We can’t wait to welcome fans once again for this great tradition that we all in Kentucky are proud to call our own.”

Another highlight of Bourbon & Beyond each year is the bourbon and culinary events, workshops, and activations that feature celebrity chefs and food and drink experts such as Chris Blandford, Amanda Freitag, Ed Lee, Chris Santos, and more.

Passes for the impending event are on sale now and can be purchased here.

PRESCOTT, AZ – Pure Imagination Music Festival – Pure Imagination, the female-led and curated, all-ages destination festival, has announced the lineup for its third edition, scheduled for May 17 – 18. The festival features headliners The Flaming Lips, along with Sylvan Esso, Tanya Tucker, The Pharcyde, Danielle Ponder, The National Parks, Sarah Shook & The Disarmers, The Irie, and more. The Friday kickoff concert lineup includes Michael Franti & Spearhead and Ponderosa Grove.

Taking place in Prescott, AZ, the festival offers old-west vibes within the Prescott National Forest. The festival grounds on Watson Lake Park boast scenic hiking trails, on-site kayaking, and a blend of nature and music. The festival is family-friendly, environmentally-forward, and offers free entry for children aged 10 and under when accompanied by a ticketed adult.

Pure Imagination features an eclectic lineup, scenic surroundings, a Night Market with artisan vendors, diverse food trucks, and activities for outdoor enthusiasts. The festival prioritizes a harmonious blend of nature and music and is known for its family-friendly and environmentally-conscious approach.

The festival, founded by Candace Devine, frontwoman of Ponderosa Grove and CEO of Further West, aims to make Prescott a hub for arts, culture, and music in the Southwest. Devine expressed excitement for this year’s festival, emphasizing the joy, wonder, and human connection that live music brings.

“I am so excited for this year’s festival! Pure Imagination has truly become a movement of wonder and joy in our community, and it feels more important than ever. In this time when the world feels a little off course, nothing brings a warmer feeling to my heart than knowing we are just a few months away from experiencing true human connection to each other, our beautiful landscape, and a greater communal kindness that comes through all of us from live music,” said Devine.

She continues, “I can’t wait to see this year’s lineup on our stages, along with the addition of the Friday night kick-off concert. What’s better than the most magical festival experience? More of it! I am so looking forward to seeing faces that I have seen the last couple of years return and seeing the many new faces that have heard about us and are joining in to add to the magic. It’s our festival family that really makes Pure Imagination as special as it is.”

A portion of the festival’s proceeds will be donated to the Flying Horse Foundation, an organization supporting arts and music in multicultural spaces.

LAS VEGAS – Lovers & Friends 2024 – Usher, Janet Jackson and The Backstreet Boys will lead the third edition of Lovers & Friends, returning to the Las Vegas Festival Grounds on Saturday (May 4). The powerhouse lineup will treat fans to performances from Lil Wayne, Snoop Dogg, Gwen Stefani, Nas, Alicia Keys, Nelly Furtado, Ludacris, Mary J. Blige, Ciara, TLC, Timbaland and many more. Special sets will include Lil Wayne performing Tha Carter III in its entirety and Usher performing Confessions, celebrating the album’s 20th anniversary.

Ticket presale begins on Friday (January 26) at 10 am PT. General admission (GA), GA+, VIP, and VIP Cabana tickets will be available, and layaway payment plans start at $19.99. VIP packages include preferred viewing areas, charging stations, dedicated entry lanes, air-conditioned restrooms, and more. VIP Cabanas offer exclusive viewing areas with VIP service, including food vouchers and select complimentary beverages.

Lovers & Friends has partnered with Jampack for hotel and ticket packages with exclusive amenities, including discount rates on select Las Vegas hotels. Public on-sale will follow if any tickets remain. For the full list of available tickets, packages, and the amenities included in each, visit the official website HERE.
