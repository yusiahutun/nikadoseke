Adrienne Bailon Slams Her &quot;Ratchet&quot; Rob Kardashian Butt Tattoo

March 22, 2024
blog

Adrienne Bailon has some words of advice to anyone wanting to get their significant other's name inked on them for life: Don't do it!

The ladies of "The Real" talked about bad tattoos on this week's show, where Adrienne didn't hold back about the "ratchet-ess booty tattoo" she got of her ex, Rob Kardashian.

That's right ... she got "Robert A. Kardashian" permanently etched to her rear end. His full name!

"Ten letter last name. Kardashian. It's pretty bad, yeah," says Adrienne. "Now that is a ratchet tattoo. I made a very bad decision. Don't follow my example."

Check out the video above to find out how it was even possible for the tattoo artist to get such a large name on her butt.

Bailon says she's currently in the process of laser tattoo removal, but says she hates going in for treatments.

"It does not feel good. You could smell your flesh burning. It's my ass flesh burning," she says. Quite the visual, right?

At least she's not alone. Rob has her name inked to his skin as well, on his ribcage.
