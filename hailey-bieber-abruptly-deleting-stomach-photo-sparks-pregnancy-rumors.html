Hailey Bieber abruptly deleting stomach photo sparks pregnancy rumors

September 09, 2024
blog

Pregnancy rumors have been surrounding Hailey Bieber for weeks, and it seems like more fuel has been added as she quickly deleted a photo of her bare stomach from her Instagram photo dump, and uploaded a new version without it.

Hailey Bieber regularly updates fans on her life via Instagram, however lately fans have been taking extra notice as they’ve been pointing out that the model has been posting much less amid rumors.

Hailey Bieber deletes belly pic

In the original photo dump, which has now been deleted, Hailey shared a photo of her belly chain which read ‘Bieber’, as her bare stomach was on show.

However, the post was then deleted, and a new version of the slideshow was uploaded, with the stomach pic noticeably missing.

Hailey captioned the re-uploaded dump: “is currently watching all of sex and the city for the first time ever,” as she was seen sporting a huge B necklace in the front pic.

Recently, Hailey has been spotted wearing baggy clothes, and covering her stomach with an iPad in pap pics, leading fans to believe the rumors could be true.

Fans notice the missing snap

Of course, eagle-eyed fans noticed the disappearance of the pic, as they took to the comments to ask where it had gone.

“The fact that she deleted the belly chain photo because of the comments is so sad. Why can’t people just STOP?” questioned one.

Fellow celebrity pal Vanessa Hudgens wrote: “This belly chain is too cute.”

However, the actress’s comment sparked confusion for some, as one wrote: “I’m confused what belly chain?”

Another wrote: “VANESSA I KNEW YOU WERE A REAL ONE”

“I would love to see Hailey Bieber walking the VS fashion show PREGNANT,” penned another.

Viral TikTok sparks Hailey pregnancy rumors even further

A video that went viral on TikTok at the end of last month sparked pregnant rumors even further as Justin and Hailey can be seen stepping out with friends.

In it, someone can be heard saying “I think, I mean, I know you are pregnant but.” The video then goes silent as Justin notices a paparazzi recording.

The 26-year-old has been seen covering her stomach with an iPad in recent pap pics which has made fans suspect the unannounced pregnancy even more, however, neither have directly commented on the rumors as of yet.
