Unisex perfume | 18 of the best gender-neutral fragrances

June 11, 2024

1

Unnamed Byredo

1

Unnamed Byredo

Back by popular request, this mysterious nameless scent gives you the reigns to decide its title and story. Note wise, this limited-edition perfume boasts a woody and warming unisex aroma, with a herbal gin and pink pepper opening, powdery yet clean violet heart and base of fir balsam and tree moss. Not to mention, it comes with a selection of stylish stickers for complete customisation on its arrival.

2

Dark Rum Eau De Parfum Malin + Goetz

2

Dark Rum Eau De Parfum Malin + Goetz

If you're looking for a fragrance that's both boozy and crisp, look to Malin + Goetz. This aromatic gourmand defies gendered fragrance conventions: its leather and rum heart is enclosed between a delicious plum and bergamot opening and amber, milk and patchouli base. It's nothing if not indulgent.

3

Morning Chess Eau de Parfum Vilhelm Parfumerie

3

Morning Chess Eau de Parfum Vilhelm Parfumerie

Inspired by summers spent battling over a chess board with his grandfather, Jan Ahlgran created this luscious citrus-green scent with notes of bergamot, leather and patchouli. It's a sophisticated sunny perfume that blurs the line between feminine and masculine.

4

Santal Greenery Eau de Parfum Dries Van Noten

4

Santal Greenery Eau de Parfum Dries Van Noten

Warming into your skin as you wear it, this long-lasting sandalwood scent is a true crowd-pleaser. Combing classic white musk with a smooth and creamy fig accord, this timeless scent story takes you on a citrus-to-green journey – perfect as we head into spring. Need we even start on the packaging?

5

By The Fireplace Eau de Toilette Maison Margiela Replica

5

By The Fireplace Eau de Toilette Maison Margiela Replica

When it comes to picturesque fragrances, nothing falls more atmospheric nor cosier than Replica's By The Fireplace. Marrying comforting cashmere with burning chestnut and guaiac wood, this scene-setting scent is quickly becoming a beloved modern classic.

6

Smoke Eau de Parfum Akro

6

Smoke Eau de Parfum Akro

Now 15% Off

Evoking the vision of a bustling smoking area on crisp London night, this alluring evening scent will captivate fans of burnt woody notes. Pairing tobacco leaves, nicotine and cade oil with rich and warming tonka bean, Akro's distinctive cigarette scent is everything you could want from spiralling smoke notes, with an unexpected hit of sweetness.

7

Violet Ends Boy Smells

7

Violet Ends Boy Smells

This beloved Boy Smells scent laces together a delicate and fresh violet with rich tanned leather, black tea and twirling trails of papyrus incense. Dynamic and complex, it's no wonder this dark yet radiant scent has earned a spot in fragrance wardrobes worldwide.

8

Orpheon Eau de Parfum Diptyque

8

Orpheon Eau de Parfum Diptyque

Credit: Space NK

An intoxicating option, Diptyque's homage to Parisian nightlife conjures an oak-lined café, filled with cigarette smoke and the clinking of sticky liqueur glasses. Juniper and jasmine are contrasted with cedar, creating a scent that feels nighttime-appropriate without becoming stuffy or cloying.

9

Cyrus Eau de Parfum Moro Dabron

9

Cyrus Eau de Parfum Moro Dabron

Credit: Selfridges

Made in small batches in the UK, Moro Dabron has taken the world of niche perfumery by storm over the last year. Its scents are steeped in literature and art (Vita is inspired by the writer and Virginia Woolf's lover Vita Sackville-West, while Downland evokes the smudgy Sussex landscapes of the painter William Nicholson). One of our favourites is Cyrus - a beguiling mix of pine, cyprus and oakmoss. Clinging to thick coats and scarves, we'll be wearing it all winter long.

10

Neroli 36 Eau de Parfum Le Labo

10

Neroli 36 Eau de Parfum Le Labo

Credit: Selfridges

For those who want to seek out something more off the beaten track than Santal 33, Neroli 36 might just be for you. The heady mix of orange blossom, musk, jasmine and vanilla makes this the perfect scent to layer with something darker (or to simply wear alone).

11

Molecule 01 Eau de Toilette Escentric Molecules

11

Molecule 01 Eau de Toilette Escentric Molecules

Credit: Liberty London

For something truly unique, it has to be this revolutionary fragrance from rule-breaking nose Geza Schön. Molecule 01 contains a single ingredient, Iso-E-Super: a synthetic note usually employed to enhance woody blends. When used alone, it blends with your natural scent to create a truly unique trail with a curious, softly musky finish that you can't quite define. You may not be able to smell it on yourself – just trust that, to others, it's intoxicating.

12

Jazmn Yucatan Eau de Parfum D.S. & Durga

12

Jazmn Yucatan Eau de Parfum D.S. & Durga

Credit: Liberty London

Really, any of D.S. & Durga's sublime scents could have featured here, but the recent Jazmin Yucatan really raises the bar.

Inspired by its namesake jungle, it's a feat of olfactory realism, with aquatic humidity warming jasmine-sweetened air, grounded by damp vetiver-laced earth. If it's escapism you're seeking, this is a must-try.

13

Bigarade Concentre Eau de Toilette Frédéric Malle

13

Bigarade Concentre Eau de Toilette Frédéric Malle

Credit: Liberty London

A minimalist concoction, this cult classic is perfect for anyone who prefers simple yet sublimely realistic notes. The true citrus blend features bitter orange you can almost taste, with a touch of rose and cedar to ground it.

14

CK All Unisex Eau de Toilette Calvin Klein

14

CK All Unisex Eau de Toilette Calvin Klein

Now 25% Off

Calvin Klein's gender-neutral scent is a modern riff on the iconic CK One, with fresh, citrus notes of mandarin and bergamot and crisp, green vetiver.

15

Ombré Leather Eau de Parfum Tom Ford

15

Ombré Leather Eau de Parfum Tom Ford

Credit: Sephora

Tom Ford's ode to his Texan roots hits a little differently to most leather-based scents, eschewing the overt machismo in favour of a softer, more supple trail. Jasmine and patchouli bring a floral twist, while cardamom leave a spicy finish.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

16

Lime Basil and Mandarin Cologne Jo Malone London

16

Lime Basil and Mandarin Cologne Jo Malone London

People often forget, but all of Jo Malone London's colognes are designed to be unisex, which is probably why they are so universally popular. Lime Basil & Mandarin remains a cult favourite for its fresh and zesty aroma.

17

Histoire d'Orangers Eau de Parfum L'Artisan Parfumeur

17

Histoire d'Orangers Eau de Parfum L'Artisan Parfumeur

Proving that feminine-leaning scents don't need to be sweet, this addictive blend sees a heart of orange blossom surrounded by warm neroli, white tea and soft musk.

18

Bois D'Hiver Eau De Parfum Ex Nihilo

18

Bois D'Hiver Eau De Parfum Ex Nihilo

Credit: Harvey Nichols

This woody scent was designed to play with the notions of softness and strength. The result is a collection of spicy yet fleeting top notes, like pink peppercorn and cardamom, which fade to reveal the dryness of patchouli and light cedarwood.
