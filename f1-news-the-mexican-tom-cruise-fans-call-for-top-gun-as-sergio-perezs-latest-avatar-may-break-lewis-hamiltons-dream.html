And now the other Red Bull driver might be the reason behind the Briton losing another dream of his. Let’s find out what Sergio Perez is up to and how he might shatter Hamilton’s dream.

Lewis Hamilton has always wanted to star in a Top Gun movie with Tom Cruise. The Briton revealed his dream and even asked Tom Cruise to give him a role in the Top Gun franchise. Hamilton was so desperate that he was ready to star as even a janitor. Tom Cruise kept his word and offered Lewis the role of a fighter pilot in Top Gun 2. Red Bull recently posted a video of Sergio Perez on Instagram where the Mexican is seen sitting on the Moto GP challenger of Marc Marquez.
