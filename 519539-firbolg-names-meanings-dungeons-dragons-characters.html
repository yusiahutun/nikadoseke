A suitable moniker for your Dungeon & Dragons characters can sometimes be challenging to create. Firbolgs are semi-giants and are derived from Irish mythology. One thing about male and female Firbolg names is their strong connection with nature, showing how simple and peaceful their lives are. The names tell of old secrets of the forest.
