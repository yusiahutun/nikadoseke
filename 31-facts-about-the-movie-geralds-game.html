Gerald’s Game is a gripping psychological horror movie that kept audiences on the edge of their seats when it was released in 2017. Directed by Mike Flanagan and based on the novel of the same name by Stephen King, this film tells the chilling story of a woman named Jessie Burlingame who finds herself trapped and handcuffed to a bed in an isolated cabin after her husband dies unexpectedly. As the hours tick by, Jessie must confront her deepest fears and inner demons in order to survive.
