Who is Andy McCauley?

Andy McCauley is a man from Hedgesville, West Virginia, convicted of murdering his girlfriend's teenage daughter, Riley Crossman, in 2019. On 4 November 2021, the jury found him guilty of the first-degree murder charge with no mercy.

What did Andy McCauley Jr do?

According to the prosecutors, Andy McCauley, in West Virginia, attacked 15-year-old Riley in her room on the night of 7 May 2019. At the time, Riley was chatting with her boyfriend, Haydn, to whom she texted that Andy was in her room and that she was scared.

Andy tried to violate Riley before killing her. He transported her body in his work truck before dumping her on the side of a road. One week later, on 16 May 2019, investigators found the decomposing body of Riley in Tuscarora Pike, Berkeley County.

Andy McCauley's trial

In September 2021, more than two years after the crime, Andy faced a week-long trial at the Morgan County Circuit Court. According to Court TV, prosecutors presented over 239 pieces of evidence plus a timeline of the events leading to Riley Crossman's disappearance and demise.

According to Beef Daily, the autopsy results indicated that Riley suffered multiple blunt-force injuries to her head and neck that ultimately led to her death. The report also revealed that there was evidence of sexual assault and that someone drugged Riley before she died.

Andy McCauley's sentencing

According to DC News Now, on 4 November 2021, the 12-person Morgan County jury found Andy guilty of all charges, including a first-degree murder charge, with no mercy and child abuse, resulting in a death.
