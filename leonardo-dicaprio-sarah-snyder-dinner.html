Leonardo DiCaprio Has Dinner with Sarah Snyder

September 28, 2024
blog

Leonardo DiCaprio was seen having dinner with model Sarah Snyder in New York City on Wednesday night. Though the two were photographed sharing a few laughs during the outing, sources said they were with "a larger group of people" that evening and are "just friends," according to Page Six.

DiCaprio, 43, covered up in a baseball cap while Snyder, 22, wore braided pigtails as they dined at Estiatorio Milos in midtown Manhattan. When the Oscar winner was spotted exiting the get-together, he left alone.

Snyder, who's posed for Hugo Boss and walked for Dolce & Gabbana, was most recently linked to Jaden Smith. The two dated for about two years, but reportedly broke up early this year after they "started growing apart."

DiCaprio broke up with Nina Agdal in March after the couple was together for almost a year. Then in August, he was rumored to be dating model Lorena Rae. The two were seen on several dates around New York City, including lunches and Citi Bike rides, but the actor's rep denied the two were dating. To confuse us even more, the actor was seen with his ex, Toni Garrn, in NYC a month later. The two had dinner on the same day DiCaprio was spotted hanging out with Rae.

DiCaprio's dinner with Snyder wasn't his only obligation on Wednesday night; earlier that evening he attended the Christie's auction for Leonardo Da Vinci's "Salvator Mundi," which became the most expensive painting ever sold at an auction, with a selling price of $450 million. Perhaps Leo was there doing research for a certain upcoming role.
