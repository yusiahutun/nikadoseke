Charlotte Olympia x Barbie

February 08, 2024
blog

There's no denying that Charlotte Olympia stands for ultra-feminine style, so who better to take inspiration from than the original 1950s fashion girl?

This week, the brand will launch a collection of shoes and clutch bags with Barbie as its muse.

The range includes bright-pink sandals with Perspex heels, pearl studs and feather boa detailing (£1,145), as well as a peach clutch bag topped with Barbie's dressing-table accessories (£1,125). For fans of the kitty flats, there are pink and black leopard-print-velvet versions, also available in children's sizes.

For serious Barbie aficionados, there is an exclusive Charlotte Olympia Barbie doll, complete with her own spider-web and leopard-print accessories.

It couldn't get much more kitsch, but we love this collaboration regardless.

The Charlotte Olympia x Barbie collection will be available to buy from 6 September exclusively at Charlotte Olympia stores, www.charlotteolympia.com, www.barbieworld.com and www.net-a-porter.com.
