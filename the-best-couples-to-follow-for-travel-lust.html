The Best Couples to Follow for Travel Lust

July 24, 2024
blog

Today is the most magical day of the year: Valentine’s Day. To celebrate this day, we thought it would be nice to highlight some of the best travel couples whose posts simply melt our hearts as well as inspire us. 

The Best Couples to Follow for Travel Lust

Raquel & Miguel

Raquel Janeiro and Miguel Mimoso, also known on Instagram as Explorerssaurus, are two Portuguese adventurers who consider the globe to be their backyard. Their Instagram account, which began in August of 2017, now has over 1,3 million followers, and over 1000 inspiring photos and endless adventures have been posted since then, many of which were compiled in their book “The adventures, tips, and secrets of the most famous travelers on Instagram,” published by Manuscrito last year.

Marie & Jake

Marie and Jake are a German-Australian couple that lives in Bali. Looking at their gorgeous photos, it’s not easy to keep your jealousy to yourself. They’re beautiful when they’re posing for a photo, sweet when they’re exercising together, and incredibly inspiring when they’re kissing somewhere magnificent. Marie once admitted that she met Jake after going through the darkest time of her life. “It showed me that happiness can be found right around the corner, no matter how horrible things are right now!” This German-Australian couple is known for their romantic photos set in beautiful, exotic locations, and their editing has helped them create a unique photo style. They have now over 1.1 followers with over 1800 posts. 

Frida & Domi

This couple appears to have traveled extensively over the world! Domi and Frida are high-end travel content providers. Their undying love for one another can easily be seen through their photos. Along with vivid photographs, they offer advice on which places to visit in a specific location, where to stay, and what to do for fun.

Hannah & Nick

Everyone is invited to see the world through Hannah and Nick’s eyes. That sounds like an offer we can’t pass up. It’s tough not to be envious of these two creatives’ lovely content. Hannah and Nick have already established themselves as one of the top social media couples and the proprietors of a fantastic couple travel blog by showing wonderful corners of the world. They also sell their photo filters to assist you to make your photos look as good as theirs.

Katie & Xavier

These two are LA-based digital content creators. Their vacation photos are true works of art with breathtaking sunsets and underwater kisses. Xavier and Katie are true role models for everybody who fantasizes about embarking on exciting journeys while holding the hand of someone special.

Kelly & Kody

Kelly and Kody’s lives are built on the foundations of adventure, creativity, positivity, and love. They travel the world, capturing their adventures and sharing them on social media, accompanied by appetizing stories. This lovely Instagram couple also merits the label of being a highly sporty and fit couple. 

Vivian & Johannes

Vivian and Johannes are a German couple who have made Bali their home. Their Instagram photos are almost certainly amazing, as can be understood by the first sentence. They offer their experiences about life in Bali in addition to stunning sites and photos to keep. We’re unable to take our gaze away from them, and how about you?

Collette & Scott

Scott and Collette are representing Mr. and Mrs. Worldwide. “A husband and wife deeply in love with each other and the world,” they describe themselves as. They’ve featured on Disney, Travel Channel, and the BBC as travel journalists and hosts. “We rented our house, resigned our jobs, and threw caution to the wind in order to see the globe and all of its delights,” Scott and Colette explain. Doesn’t it sound amazing? This travel couple’s perspective of our earth is breathtaking.

Sydney & Davis

Davis and Sydney Hansen are a traveling couple with a strong interest in photography and videography. They motivate people by displaying the beauty of the earth through their travel content. Their online moniker is The Escape Artists since they combined their passion for art and travel. The Escape Artists began their journey while sitting in a little cafe in the south of France. They’re now traveling the world with their camera in order to keep their sense of wonder alive!

Judith & Mark

This Dutch couple is traveling around the world and documenting every step they take. They identify themselves as creative storytellers. They post their adventures and beautiful moments from their travels on their Instagram page as well as their website called Travel Monster.

Who is your favorite couple from this list? Would you like to embark on a journey just like theirs? Tell us your thoughts down below!
