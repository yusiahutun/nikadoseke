Sharon Osbourne's latest facelift was the 'worst thing she ever did'

September 18, 2024
blog

Sharon Osbourne has spoken candidly about having another facelift and regrets having it done.

The celebrity said the facelift she had done in October 2021 made her “look like a f***ing Cyclops” and said she had to have corrective surgery to fix what it had done to her face. Sharon has had numerous surgeries over the years, and the 70-year-old admitted she is done with having them.

Sharon Osbourne says she regrets her last facelift

Talking to The Sun recently, the music manager said after having the latest operation two years ago, her eyes didn’t look symmetrical on her face. The star felt she looked like a mummy with the amount of bandages she had on her face to help with the healing.

“That one put me off and it frightens me. I really f***ing pushed it with the last facelift, and I am now like, no more,” Sharon admitted. “Time is against me, I cannot have another facelift.”

This isn’t the first time that Sharon has opened up about what happened after the five-hour-long surgery she had. She told The Sunday Times in 2022: “I had a full facelift done in October and I looked like one of those f—ing mummies that they wrap [with bandages]. It hurt like hell. You have no idea. One eye was different to the other. I looked like a f***ing Cyclops. I’m, like, ‘All I need is a hunchback.'”

Upon seeing the results, she said the procedure hadn’t been worth the amount it cost. The X Factor Judge turned to the surgeon who performed it and asked: “‘You’ve got to be f***ing joking.'”

Even her rock star husband, Ozzy Osbourne, was so horrified by what had transpired. He told her: “I don’t care how much it costs, we’ll get it redone.”

This isn’t the first time Sharon has said she will stop

According to People, 11 years ago when hosting the TV show The Talk she said she was giving up making changes to her body such as her face and breasts, saying there would be “no more abuse.”

She explained: “No more, because I have been looking at pictures of myself recently since I started to lose weight. And in a lot of shots, my face looks plastic and at certain angles I was like, ‘Oh, dear. Oh, I should never have done that. Oh, that’s a bad one.’ So I’m like, ‘No more. No more abuse.’”

She also said one of the implants in her breasts burst. “One of them had burst, and all of it had gone into the wall of my stomach. And one breast was different than the other.”

After a surgery seven years later, she couldn’t feel her face

Despite saying she wouldn’t be going under the knife ever again, Sharon Osbourne had a facelift in 2019. While appearing on the TV show The Talk she was still in “a little bit of pain” but “couldn’t complain” as she wanted to have it done.

“It was five weeks today that I had my operation. I had my neck done, my jowls … He kind of pulled it from the top of my head and put an elastic band in it. But everything was just lifted up. So it looks more refreshed. I feel okay. Still a little bit of pain. But you can’t complain when you wanted to do it.”

She couldn’t feel her mouth after having the operation, and told Kelly Clarkson she “looked like Elvis.”

She explained: “I had this thing where they lifted up my mouth and then for the first week I couldn’t feel my mouth. I can hardly feel my mouth now, to be honest with you,” Osbourne said. “I couldn’t find my mouth. It was numb and it was up on one side and I looked like Elvis.

“All the kids and Ozzy are going ‘Why are you snarling at me’ and I’m like ‘I’m not snarling. I’m not doing anything!'”
