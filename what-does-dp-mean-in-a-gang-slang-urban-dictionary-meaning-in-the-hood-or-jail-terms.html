What Does DP Mean In Jail Or Prison Terms?

The meaning of DP in jail or prison terms is Diversion Programs, also known as a pretrial diversion program or pretrial intervention program in the criminal justice system.

 Other prison acronyms are DHO- Disciplinary Hearing Officer, DHS- Department of Human Services, DM- Decision Memorandum, BOP- Biennial Operating Plan, BP- Board Policy, BPP- Board of Pardons and Paroles, BR- Board Rule, CA- County Attorney. 

Besides, the meaning of Dp in LA is Designated Player Rule.
