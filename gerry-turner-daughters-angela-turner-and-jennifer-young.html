The Golden Bachelor: Who Are Gerry Turner Daughters?

Gerry Turner the leading man on The Golden Bachelor was previously married and shared two daughters from his previous marriage.

The 71-year-old’s wife was named Toni Turner, his high school sweetheart. They were married for 43 years until Toni’s passing in 2017. Toni was born on 22 November 1951 and she died on 15 July 2017. Gerry also mentioned his wife ahead of the show’s premiere.

“I have her picture on a dresser in my closet,” he shared of his late wife. “And every morning I give her the nod, ‘So what do you think about this?’ For a while, I was having a hard time figuring out if she would be okay.”

He continued, “But we always told each other when one of us goes we want the other to be happy. She’s up there rooting, she’s saying, ‘Yeah Gerry, do this.'”

And as Gerry revealed, it was his adult daughters who prompted his journey to finding love again as they were the ones who signed him up. He said he felt like there was “nothing to lose.” So, what is he looking for in a partner?

“I’d love it if I found a partner who was high-energy,” he revealed. “Someone that maybe plays pickleball, someone that maybe plays golf.”

Gerry has been announced as the main character in The Golden Bachelor, the newest entry in the Bachelor franchise. The 71-year-old will be followed in this series as he meets ladies who are looking for love in their senior years, in contrast to The Bachelor and Bachelor in Paradise, which center on people in their 20s and 30s.
