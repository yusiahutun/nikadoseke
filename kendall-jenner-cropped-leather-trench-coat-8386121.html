Kendall Jenners Cropped Leather Trench Coat Is Fall Outerwear Done Right

February 04, 2024
blog

As the temperatures begin to dip and the holiday season nears, we have no choice but to embrace outerwear season. And Kendall Jenner's already on it.

On Wednesday, after a parade of incredible Halloween costumes, the supermodel stepped out for a coffee run, sporting an oversized brown leather cropped trench coat. While stopping by Alfred Coffee in Los Angeles's Studio City neighborhood, Kendall wore the luxe leather jacket with a coordinating belt wrapped around her waist. She teamed the blazer-length coat with nothing but a matching pencil skirt and a pair of black leather loafers. Meanwhile, tortoiseshell sunglasses, orange drop earrings, and a lime green handbag provided the finishing touches to her OOTD.

Beauty-wise, her manicure expertly coordinated with her chocolate brown ‘fit, and she styled her long brunette hair into tousled beach-y waves and a middle part. She opted for a no-makeup-makeup look with rosy cheeks and a glossy pink pout.

Earlier this week, the supermodel ditched her brown locks and went blonde for Halloween (now that's a serious commitment). Ahead of her annual Halloween party, Kendall posted a series of snaps to Instagram documenting her spot-on recreation of Marilyn Monroe's memorable 1953 Life magazine photo shoot. The costume included a black Intimissimi turtleneck, white cigarette pants, diamond chandelier earrings, and a curly blonde bob wig.

In the caption, she referenced another Marilyn moment, writing: "Happy birthday, mister president."
