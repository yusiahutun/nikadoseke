Ex-Barcelona star Neymar is seemingly no longer in a relationship with Bruna Biancardi after he was exposed allegedly asking an adult content creator for nudes.

"This is a private matter, but as I am exposed to news, assumptions and jokes on a daily basis, I inform you that I am not in a relationship," Biancardi announced on Instagram, just weeks after giving birth to the couple's baby daughter. The news comes soon after it emerged Al-Hilal forward Neymar was allegedly caught asking Aline Faria for explicit images.

That development was all the more surprising given it came just weeks after Biancardi, 29, bore a daughter, Mavie. And Biancardi is eager to keep a healthy relationship with the newborn's father, despite his purported misbehaviour.
