Winter should be fun! The Winter House cast for Season 3 includes stars from six Bravo shows including Summer House, Vanderpump Rules and more.

Winter House is Bravo’s reality TV series following a group of Bravo stars from various shows as they vacation in the snow. The series, which is a spin-off of Bravo’s Summer House set in The Hamptons, New York, premiered in 2021 and has since featured more than a dozen Bravo stars from across shows like Southern Charm and Below Deck.

The Winter House cast for Season 3 includes eight main cast members from six Bravo shows: Vanderpump Rules, Summer House, Below Deck Mediterranean, Below Deck Sailing Yacht, Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard, and Family Karma. According to Entertainment Tonight, Vanderpump Rules star Tom Sandoval was set to be a part of the Winter House cast for Season 3, however, he was cut after news broke of his affair—now known as Scandoval—involving his ex-girlfriend, Ariana Madix, and their co-star, Raquel Leviss. The site also reported that Tom Schwartz, Sandoval’s Vanderpump Rules co-star, also almost missed Winter House filming due to Scandoval, which required cameras to pick back up for Vanderpump Rules Season 10.

“There is a possibility the duo could go to Colorado later, and come in near the end of the Winter House season. Producers are now reaching out to some of the previous Winter House cast to see if they can join, and asking part-time people to now be full-time,” a source told Entertainment Tonight in March 2023.
