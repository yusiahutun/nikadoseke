What does purple shampoo do to hair?

.

If light hair starts to oxidize, it can turn yellow or, as we in the biz say, brassy. “The purple removes that brassiness to keep the blonde and silver colors truer,” explains Brown.

How long does it take purple shampoo to work?

.

Instantly. One wash, and you'll notice a difference. On the same token, it's easy to overdo it; most colorists and hairstylists recommend swapping it in for your regular shampoo once a week, but you can gauge usage depending on your product, hair type, and desired brightness.
