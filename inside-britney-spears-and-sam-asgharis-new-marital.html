A few days after their wedding, multiple sources reported that the newlyweds had moved into a brand new mansion located in the posh Calabasas area in Los Angeles.

We take a look inside Mr and Mrs Asghari’s jaw-dropping gorgeous mansion below.

Who is the richest woman in the world right now? (No, it’s not Kim K)

Their new mansion costs US$11.8 million

Sitting on a sprawling 0.6 hectares (1.6 acres) of land, their new Calabasas mansion takes up 11,650 sq ft of the total area. The contemporary Mediterranean single-storey home is built with tile, clay and concrete.

Coldwell Banker listed the original price at US$11.8 million on its site. The official description described Spears’ new estate as such: “Strict security in a double-gated community. The estates of The Oaks, spectacular, private, one level, custom-built and one-of-a-kind masterpieces.”

Seven bedrooms, nine baths, and four garages (plus a gift-wrapping room)

If the estate’s facade has already taken your breath away, wait until you look at what’s inside. According to the property listing site, the pop diva’s new home boasts seven bedrooms, nine bathrooms, a heated pool, a home gym, a plush cinema, a wine cellar and separate maid’s quarters. But the most unique space in this expansive mansion might be the dedicated gift-wrapping room!

Each bathroom has a sunken tub, double vanity and powder room, while the house also comes with its own heated spa located in-ground with his-and-hers dressing areas in the primary suite.

Spears’ bedroom has a fireplace and a striking arch entrance designed in beautiful natural hues of white, beige and grey. There is also a comfortable seating area next to the master bed decorated with a crystal chandelier, exuding a luxurious yet homey atmosphere.

Meanwhile, the sprawling kitchen is located next to the spacious living room and comes with a walk-in pantry, dining area and breakfast bar. You can also find relaxing outdoor dining and living areas that overlook the manicured gardens on the property.

As we know, the Toxic singer also loves her supercars. Here, she can keep them in the safe harbour of her four parking spaces. The garages come with built-in storage, a brick and gated driveway, a golf cart and an oversized driveway, according to the official listing.

Don’t forget the OTT high-ceilinged foyer ….

The breathtaking Calabasas mansion also features OTT amenities and opulent interiors – we expected no less from Spears.

The main living room’s chief draw is the jaw-dropping 10-metre-high (33-foot) ceiling foyer embellished with precious carved stones. This is followed by wide-arched hallways throughout the house with custom-made chandeliers. The abode’s mounted walls also come with matching candelabras and hand-carved woods, too.

Meanwhile, a majestic fountain overlooking the mosaic tiled pools is neatly placed outside. The newlyweds can also enjoy a huge oval jacuzzi, multiple cascading waterfalls, water slides and an outdoor barbecue pavilion for a fun-filled day in the sun.

Her new place is located near ex Kevin Federline’s home

The most interesting fact about the new house might be that it’s located near the home of Spears’ ex-husband Kevin Federline, where he lives with their two sons, Sean Preston and Jayden James.

“Kevin was surprised that, of all the neighbourhoods she can afford to move to, she chose his,” a source told TMZ. “As long as his peace and tranquillity aren’t invaded as a result, more power to her.”

The property was listed in January 2021, but it was actually built in 2009. The Coldwell Banker Homes site stated that “this property was originally built as an owner user with unparalleled quality and design” … and we have to agree!
