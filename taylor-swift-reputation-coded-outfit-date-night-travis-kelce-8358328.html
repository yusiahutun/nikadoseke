Taylor Swift Wore a 'Reputation'-Coded Outfit for Date Night With Travis Kelce

June 30, 2024
blog

Another day, another Traylor sighting. After stepping out hand-in-hand to attend the Saturday Night Live afterparty this weekend, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce were spotted on another date in New York City the next day — this time, grabbing dinner at the West Village's Waverly Inn. 

For the occasion, Swift seemingly embraced her Reputation era in an unexpectedly edgy outfit. On top, the singer wore a black bustier underneath a long-sleeved black sheer top that featured a pink and green floral pattern, which she teamed with a see-through shirt, a black leather miniskirt, and a pair of lace-up combat boots with a chunky heel (also in black). She accessorized with tiny diamond hoops and a burgundy shoulder bag. 

When it came down to glam, Taylor swapped her signature red lip for a light pink shade, and added a dusting of copper-colored eyeshadow to her lids. Her hair, on the other hand, channeled her evermore period with her wavy strands pulled back into a messy bun with face-framing pieces and eyebrow-grazing fringe.

Meanwhile, Kelce opted for a light beige corduroy two-piece set that consisted of an oversized button-down shirt and baggy pants. He wore a black T-shirt underneath, and finished off his outfit with a pair of white Nike sneakers. The football star adorably wrapped both of his hands around Swift's, guiding her to the car through the swarm of paparazzi and fans.  

As for all the publicity surrounding their relationship, it doesn't bother Travis. “It comes with it,” he said during a press conference earlier this month. “You’ve got a lot of people that care about Taylor, and for good reason. You just have to keep living and learning and enjoying the moments.”
