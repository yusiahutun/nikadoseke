Kate Beckinsale fans send well-wishes as movie star pictured in wheelchair

August 12, 2024
blog

Kate Beckinsale’s latest life update is making fans concerned as they pour wishes for the recovery of the actress.

The Aviator star Kate Beckinsale’s latest Instagram post is a clapback to all the rumors that the movie star is going under the knife to fix her face or to remain youthful. The 50-year-old celebrity has been vocal about the plastic surgery rumors before. She replied to a fan back in May: “No threads, no thread lift, no Botox, no laser, no nose job, no filler” talking about how she is ‘this gorgeous’ at 50.

Kate Beckinsale concerns fans with wheelchair photo

Kate Beckinsale has gone through surgery which made her end up in a wheelchair. But it is not what the followers think it is.

The Jolt star updated her 5 million followers on Instagram that she has gone through dental surgery. She wrote: “Just for balance and to answer a question I am sometimes asked “do you ever take a bad picture” may I direct you to the absolute D*** MAGNET that left the dentist surgery this morning.”

Beckinsale’s post prompted fans to send her well wishes but one comment riled her up. It read: “You don’t need work done to your face. This is a bad example to young girls growing up saying you can’t age gracefully”.

The actress replied: “PSA to all young women do not get your wisdom teeth removed because this b***end doesn’t like it. “

The secret behind Beckinsale’s beauty

Kate Beckinsale has been ‘fighting’ against the plastic surgery rumors for a while. The actress has been questioned what sort of ‘work’ she has done on her face multiple times to which the star gave a befitting reply revealing her skincare routine which does not require a ‘facelift.’

“Please tell me you had a facelift because it’s not possible to be this gorgeous at 50,” asked a fan.

But she replied denied doing any botox or treatment. Kate said: “I do have facials with PRP to boost collagen and microcurrent for skin tightening and oxygen.”
