Popular content creator Baba Talisha has heaped praise on his parents who are celebrating their 40th marriage anniversary on April 8, 2024.

Parents are Baba Talisha's support system

In a beautiful Facebook post showing the photos of the loving parents, Baba Talisha, wished them a good time and asked them to enjoy their day.

Speaking to TUKO.co.ke in an interview, Baba Talisha revealed that his parents struggled so much to take care of them and ensure they were well provided for.

"They have been our support system, they have always provided for us; I have seen them struggle but still provide for us," said Baba Talisha.

Parents don't fight, says Baba Talisha

"Since I was born, I have never seen my parents have issues like fighting etc. They have always prayed and showed us Christian ethics," he added.

In some families, some children may feel that their parents love their siblings more than they do, but Baba Talisha maintains that they were raised in a loving environment.

"They have always loved us equally," he said.

Once netizens saw the photo of the matronly lady smiling lovingly into her husband's face, they joined Baba Talisha in wishing the best to his parents.
