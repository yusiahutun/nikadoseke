1. Ditching phones all-day

You and your partner will ditch your phones for a day. This means that none of you will make a phone call, send text messages, or go to social media. The one who will pick up their phone to make a call or send a text message will lose the bet. The loser has to prepare breakfast for the winner.

2. Dating reality TV shows

Watch a dating reality television show of your choice. Each of you should guess who will end up with whom or who will be eliminated that week. The one who makes the correct prediction wins. On the other hand, the loser has to do all the house chores.

3. Who can peel the boiled eggs faster

You and your partner should divide hard-boiled eggs equally between the two of you. Start peeling the eggs. The first one to finish is the winner of the game, and he/she will decide where both of you are going for a dinner date.

4. Who can stand on one leg for longer?

If you're both enjoy physical challenges or love exercising together, then you can incorporate this activity into your bet. The one who will balance on one leg for longer is the winner. Whoever loses will have to drive the winner around for an entire day at the winner's request.

5. Choose if a coin will land on the head or tail

You and your partner should bet if a coin lands on the head or tail. Throw the coin in the air and let it land on the ground. The one who made the correct prediction is the winner. The loser will be required to wash the winner's car for one week.

6. Who can put the child to sleep fast?

If you and your partner are having a small child, you should each try to put the kid to bed and estimate the amount of time that you take to put the child to sleep. The one who spends less time is the winner. The loser will control the remote for the next one week.

7. In search of treasure

You and your partner will each write a list of items that the other person needs to find in the house. Then, exchange the lists and start searching. The winner of this bet is the one who finds most or all of the items on the other person's list. The one who loses should give the winner a gentle shoulder massage and foot rub.

8. Spending challenge

You and your partner should set aside a specific amount of money for each person to carry during the week. No one should be allowed to use a debit or credit card to make the challenge enjoyable. At the end of the week, whoever has the most money will be declared the winner, and he/she gets to choose the vacation destination.

9. Take a banknote from underneath the glass without spilling the water

Put a banknote underneath a glass that is full of water. You should challenge your partner to take the note from underneath the glass without spilling the water. If he/she spills the water, he should join in on a TikTok dance video.

10. Blindfolding

You should blindfold your partner and ask them to find something hidden in the wardrobe. If they find it, you are the loser. You will create a scrapbook that features the timeframe of your relationship.

Bets to make with your crush

Having some healthy competition with your crush is one of the ways to increase intimacy. You'll be able to find out new things about each other and bond in an entirely fun way. Here are some good bets to make with a girl.

1. Bet on the weather

You and your crush should predict what the weather will be like the following day without checking the news or using weather apps. The one with the most accurate predictions wins. If you lose, you will be required to do a spontaneous activity that the winner chooses.

2. Bet on a sports game

You should pick one game that is going to air on television. Each of you should predict the team that is going to win the match. At the end of the game, the winner who makes the correct prediction is declared. The loser should grant the winner three wishes.

3. The stone face

This challenge is all about stamina. You and your crush should put on a stone-faced expression without laughing. To make it more interesting, you can play some funny videos. The one who laughs first loses the bet, and they should buy the winner snacks for the entire week.

4. Food challenge

If you and your crush both love food, you should include a food challenge in the bet. Both of you should place some food on the plate and try eating without using your hands. The one who finishes their food first is the winner. The loser should kiss the winner.

5. Predict the end of the movie

If both of you love movies, you should watch a movie together. Each of you should predict how the movie will end. The winner is the one who makes the correct prediction. The loser has to wear something the winner chooses.

6. Play pool

Pool is a super fun card game that can be played with just two people, which makes it a fun game to introduce to your relationship. The loser must record a personalized alarm for the winner.

7. Staring contest

You and your crush should look into each other's eyes without blushing. The first person to blush loses the bet, and he/she will take the winner for shopping.

8. Never have I ever

You should ask your crush to mention five things they have never done. If she mentions anything you have ever done on this list, you need to interrupt and speak up. She loses, and you win. The loser will send a handwritten love letter via e-mail.

9. Play video games together

You should play a video game together with your crush. At the end of the game, the winner should be treated to something nice such as dinner or a box of chocolates.

10. Who can hold a plank for the longest?

A good bet would be any physical challenge you both enjoy and feel you could win. For example, you and your crush should each hold a plank, and the one who holds it for the longest time becomes the winner. The loser has to wear a cosplay costume all day.

These bets to make with your boyfriend should be on top of your list if you want to promote healthy competition in your relationship and increase intimacy. You can go for the simple things such as the outcome of a match, or the ending of the favourite reality show that you both love watching.
