Everyone must have passed through the hands of a teacher at some point in their lives, which makes this profession a unique one. Teachers devote their lives to making sure they make the lives of others better by offering them enlightenment. Yet, most of the time, they are underappreciated and seldom receive recognition for their contribution to society. Retirement quotes for teachers come at the end of teachers’ service to thank them for their achievements.

The work done by teachers is not easy because they receive students when they are green and have to shape them into intelligent beings, and this calls for a retirement message for a teacher's celebration.

The challenge is that different students have diverse needs, and others can be cheeky or even outright undisciplined. Therefore, tutors are called to be patient and only get rewarded when they see their students succeed in life.
