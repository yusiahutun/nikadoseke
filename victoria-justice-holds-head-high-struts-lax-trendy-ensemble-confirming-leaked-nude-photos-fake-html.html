Victoria Justice held her head high as she strutted into LAX for a flight on Tuesday.

After being caught up in the nude photo leak scandal that has swept the internet, the 21-year-old swiftly denied that the image in question was of her and got on with business, enjoying the Labor
Day long weekend before catching her flight out of town.

And with her hair beautifully coiffed and make-up impeccably applied, not to mention a funky outfit
to boot, she gave onlookers plenty more to talk about than some bogus nude photo.
