The artist started his music career by chance. After dropping out of high school due to poverty, he stayed home. He remained unemployed for a couple of years. As he matured, he started fending for himself. He made money by cleaning the streets and peddling drugs.

The young artist started creating music and uploaded it on SoundCloud and YouTube. These platforms earned him some recognition. However, his breakthrough came in August 2017 after the release of the jam Betrayed.

Betrayed peaked at number 64 on the Billboard Hot 100, and the RIAA gave it platinum certification. A short while later, the rapper announced the release of his debut album, Total Xanarchy.
