Loose Women was thrown into a spot of chaos as Jane Moore lobbed an item across the panel at Coleen Nolan.

The hit ITV show made its return to TV screens on Monday (June 26) with Charlene White, Jane, Coleen Nolan and Brenda Edwards back on the panel.

One of the hot topics on the programme today, was whether or not parents should help out with their children’s homework.

“Did you help your girls?” Charlene asked Jane.

“I was quite disorganised, you know when they wake up in the morning and they go ‘oh by the way mum it’s Victorian day’ and it’s like what?!”

She went on: “We have nothing.”

Coleen, who is no stranger to a cheeky dig, then chimed in and issued a brutal blow at her co-star.

Coleen said: “You must have clothes left from that era Jane.”

The TV star’s dig left her co-stars and the Loose Women audience bursting out in laughter, as Brenda quipped: “Oi!”

But Jane wasn’t having it and straight away she picked up something from the table and lobbed it across the panel at Coleen - who dodged the item quickly.

As the audience carried on laughing, Charlene attempted to take reins to try and swiftly move on with Monday's show.

Elsewhere on the episode, the panel discussed the news that Sarah Ferguson, the Duchess of York and former wife of Prince Andrew, has been diagnosed with cancer after a routine mammogram.

The 63-year-old was released from hospital yesterday, June 25, and is now recovering at home with her family in Windsor, Berkshire.
