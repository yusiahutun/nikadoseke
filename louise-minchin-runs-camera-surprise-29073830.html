Louise Minchin runs off camera as surprise BBC Breakfast return goes all wrong

August 12, 2024
blog

Ex- BBC Breakfast presenter Louise Minchin admitted she was “nervous” as she returned to the beloved morning show alongside co-host Naga Munchetty.

Hosting the BBC programme alongside Charlie Stayt on Saturday (January 28), fan-favourite Naga welcomed viewers back to the signature red couch as usual.

But fans were over the moon to see the return of beloved TV presenter Louise as she surprised the duo by joining them on the show once again.

But the newsreader made an unexpected appearance this morning while interviewing Dame Katherine Grainger during a charity sports event for the late rugby star Doddie Weir.

As they introduced the segment, the pair welcomed Katherine onto the show who was reporting live from the special regatta in the heart of Edinburgh in his memory.

The event celebrated the life of the former sportsman who recently died from motor neuron disease last November.

After answering a few questions about the event, Naga was interrupted as Louise jumped into the shot to catch up with the cast and crew.

Welcoming the former I'm A Celeb star onto the show, Katherine remarked: “Look who’s here” as Naga added: “Oh, hello Miss Minchin!”

The star responded: “Hi Naga, hi Charlie” before walking out of shot unable to hear their questions from the studio.

But Katherine insisted that she returned as she reassured her: “I can speak for them – how are you?” as she placed an arm round Louise.

As the dynamic duo caught up with the former morning presenter, Louise claimed she was “nervous” as she chatted to her former colleagues before admitting she “misses the sofa”.

She said: “I’m very nervous. I’m here to raise money, obviously, but I'm very nervous because I don’t know how to row and I've got about 10 minutes to learn!”

“Yes, but you’re very fit, we were just discussing how easy it is to transfer over” Katherine assured her.

“I don’t know, I'm fit here,” she replied touching her heart area, “And my legs are probably fit, but it’s going to be hard.”

She went on to apologise for her interruption, saying: “Sorry to interrupt and everything, but I just wanted to say hello, good morning and thank you very much for talking to us about this because it’s such a fun day and brilliant to be here.”

Katherine stated: “The sofa misses you” to which Louise confessed: “I miss the sofa!”
