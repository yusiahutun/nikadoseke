She was required to wear a skintight wetsuit throughout most of The Hunger Games: Catching
Fire to portray heroine Kaitness Everdeen.

And it seems Jennifer Lawrence and her fellow cast members were pleasantly surprised just how comfortable the waterproof attire was during shoots of the latest installment of the science fiction franchise.

The 23-year-old discussed the issue of 'camel toe' during an interview with E! news last week.

No camel toe here! Jennifer Lawrence says the tight wetsuit she wore in the Hunger Games: Catching Fire was actually quite comfortable in the end

'I was surprised at how little camel toe problem there was. I was expecting a lot more,' she admitted.

Meanwhile, her co-star Josh Hutcherson revealed that he would often relieve himself in the waterproof suit as the cast and crew filmed in the ocean.

'Honestly, I was expecting them to be horrible because the idea of being in a wetsuit for a whole movie sounds terrifying,' he told the website.

No problems here: The actress says the issue of 'camel toe' was not as worrisome as she thought

'But they actually were pretty comfortable once we found out you can actually pee through them—just go into the ocean and take care of your business.'

The male members of the cast wore board shorts under their tight wetsuits in an effort to avoid any visible panty lines, but initially they were asked by the wardrobe department to wear G-string underwear.

'We tried cups in the beginning, but they looked ridiculous,' Hutcherson told E!

Meanwhile Sam Claflin, who portays Finnick Odair, says they almost wore the thongs to avoid any 'visible panty line'.

No time to stop: Meanwhile, Josh Hutcherson said they would often 'pee' through their suits

'It was so there was no VPL,'  he said. 'We eventually went with cycling shorts and that kept it all in tact.'

But not all of the men opted for undergarments, with Jena Malone, who plays Johanna Mason, revealing that some went commando underneath the suits.

'Some people were free-balling it,' she told E!.

New 'do: Last week, Jennifer revealed her new short pixie haircut

Jennifer and the rest of the Hunger Games cast are currently on a press tour around the US to promote the second installment to the hit movie franchise.

Ahead of her media commitments beginning earlier last week, Jennifer shocked her fans by cutting her shoulder length honey-hued locks into a short pixie crop.

Jennifer's new look is a big departure from the long dark hair she often sports to play Katniss in her Hunger Games franchise. 

On tour: Josh Hucherson, Elizabeth Banks and Sam Claflin attended a photocall held at The Corinthia Hotel, 10 Whitehall Place, London on Monday

Goofing around: Josh and Elizabeth fooled around as they had their picture taken

  

This year she had been alternating between her natural honey-hue and the chocolate locks before wrapping filming on the movie recently.

During a Q&A last Wednesday, Jennifer explained that she cut her hair after having to dye it all the time.

That is before she joked about loping the locks off just to annoy Hunger Games director Francis Lawrence.

'I just did it to make Francis mad,' she joked. 'I did it just to annoy him. He totally thought it was me giving him the finger.

'But it's not. It's just fried from being dyed so much.'
