If you want to dive into the world of Harley Quinn movies, knowing how to watch them is essential. The character of Harley Quinn, portrayed by Margot Robbie, has appeared in several films within the DC Extended Universe. The Harley Quinn character was created for Batman: The Animated Series, making her first appearance in the 1992 episode Joker's Favour. Quinn, originally known as Harleen Quinzel, was initially intended to be a one-dimensional character.

She quickly evolved into a more complex and relatable figure for fans. Harley also became a more independent and self-aware character, which enticed fans to watch movies where she has been featured. So, how do you watch Harley Quinn movies in order?

1. Suicide Squad (2016)

Suicide Squad is the first movie with Harley Quinn and Joker. The movie follows a group of imprisoned supervillains recruited by a secret government agency to execute a dangerous mission in exchange for reduced sentences.

The story unfolds as Officer Amanda Waller, played by Viola Davis, assembles a team of some of the most notorious villains. The squad includes Deadshot, portrayed by Will Smith, Harley Quinn, the grotesque and cannibalistic Killer Croc, the possessed Katana, and several other characters.

In Suicide Squad, Harley is portrayed as the Joker's girlfriend and a former psychiatrist who has gone insane. The film accurately captures her unpredictability, vulnerability, and uncontrollable devotion to the Joker.

Quinn's character development throughout the movie is a focal point as audiences witness her evolution from a psychiatrist, Dr. Harleen Quinzel, to the lovable yet disturbed Harley Quinn.

Quinn was a psychiatrist at Arkham Asylum, where she fell in love with the Joker and ultimately became his partner-in-crime. In Suicide Squad, she is brought in as a squad member because of her unparalleled skills in combat, acrobatics, and cerebral understanding of the criminal mind.

The film also explores her troubled past, her relationship with the Joker, and her choices as a member of the Suicide Squad.

3. The Suicide Squad (2021)

If you're still watching Harley Quinn movies in order, the Suicide Squad is the third movie sequel to the 2016 Suicide Squad. It follows a group of supervillains who can redeem themselves by joining Task Force X.

This secret government agency sends them on dangerous missions in exchange for reduced sentences. The film is set in Belle Reve, a high-security prison where the worst supervillains are kept.

Task Force X assembles a team of cons, including Bloodsport, Peacemaker, Captain Boomerang, Ratcatcher 2, Savant, King Shark, Blackguard, Javelin, and Harley Quinn.

The team is sent on a mission to destroy a prison in Corto Maltese, where they must infiltrate a hidden enemy research facility called Jotunheim and ensure they destroy everything and leave no traces behind. However, they soon find themselves in a much larger conflict.

How many movies is Margot Robbie Harley Quinn?

Margot Robbie has portrayed Harley in three movies. These movies are:

These movies are connected in the same timeline and feature Margot Robbie's character portrayal of Harley Quinn.

What are the Harley Quinn and Joker movies called?

What movie does Harley Quinn fall into acid?

Quinn falls into a vat of chemicals, not acid, in the movie Suicide Squad (2016). The scene shows her origin story, where she jumps into the vat of chemicals after being pushed by the Joker. The chemicals change her appearance and drive her insane, transforming her into Harley Quinn.
