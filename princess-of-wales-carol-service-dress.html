The Princess of Wales wears red sequin dress for carol service announcement

July 28, 2024
blog

The Princess of Wales is inspiring us to embrace all things festive in our wardrobes, as she dressed up in sequins for a video to promote her special Christmas carol service.

In a video of the royal which was released this weekend, Kate was seen wearing a sequinned red gown, standing in front of a beautifully decorated Christmas tree. Royal fashion fans may recognise her shimmering dress, as she previously wore it during a state reception in January 2020.

ITV

Her mid-length red Needle & Thread dress has sheer sleeves, delicate floral appliques and ruffled detailing on the collar and shoulders. The princess wore her hair styled in her signature loose curls and finished her ensemble with a pair of red Gianvito Rossi heels and Soru ruby earrings.

Of course, Kate so often wears pieces from her wardrobe for multiple engagements, rather than always choosing to buy something new, and she proves that this should be the case not just for capsule wardrobe basics, but also for those special party pieces.

YUI MOK//Getty Images

In the released promo, Kate shared more details about the special event she is hosting this year.

"Join us this Christmas Eve for a very special service at Westminster Abbey, full of festive carols and beautiful performances, as we celebrate what makes this the most wonderful time of year," she said.

The palace also released further information on the event, which will pay homage to the life and legacy of the late Queen Elizabeth II, who died aged 96 on 8 September.

"The carol service will celebrate and showcase the joy that human connection can bring: forging friendships and strengthening communities, creating legacies of humility and kindness, whilst also providing relief and comfort in times of difficulty or loss. The service will combine both traditional and modern elements, reaching people of all faiths and none."

The service will include carols performed by the abbey's choir, while other members of the royal family, armed forces personnel, charity workers and volunteers will also be in attendance.

The Princess of Wales' royal Christmas service will take place on 15 December and will be broadcast on ITV.
