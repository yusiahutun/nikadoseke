Dr. Barbara Sturm Nightly Skin Care Routine

March 23, 2024
blog

You've probably heard of Dr. Barbara Sturm's luxury skin care and wellness line, but did you know the brand's namesake uses 12 beauty products (including a face roller) in her nightly skin care routine? In this episode of Go to Bed with Me, Sturm shares all of her tips and tricks when it comes to pampering her face at the end of the day, from using a Joanna Czech facial massager to employing a $250 sonic toothbrush.

You can shop all of her favorite products (including some from her brand) below.

Shop Dr. Barbara Sturm's Routine

"When you take off your makeup, try to avoid makeup wipes or strong makeup remover just because it's not good for your skin," Sturm advises in the beginning of the episode. "First of all, there's ingredients in the makeup wipes, which can disrupt your skin barrier function, but you're also rubbing the dirt and the makeup into your skin, and you could really destroy your skin."

Instead of makeup wipes, Sturm gives these instructions: "You just put a little bit of your face cream on a cotton pad … Take it off like this, super gentle. And then you can wash it off."
