Soleil Moon Frye Shows Off Hot Bod in Hot Pink Swimsuit, Talks 40-Pound Weight Loss!

August 16, 2024
blog

Soleil Moon Frye is looking (and feeling) better than ever!

The mother-of-three was spotted showing off her hot new bod while rocking a pink swimsuit on vacation in Lake Tahoe with her family.

Frye -- who dropped a whopping 40-pounds earlier this year with the help of Nutrisystem -- has been enjoying every moment of her summer, thanks in large part to her recent weight loss success.

"For me, I just want to feel great," she says of her transformation. "I think it's important to empower ourselves. We have kids and we love our kids. But we still have ourselves. And I want to feel like the best version of myself."

"I hope that my weight loss success will inspire other mothers to make time for themselves and take that first step toward living a happier, healthier life," she reveals.

The brunette beauty teamed up with the weight loss company after welcoming third child, son Lyric Sonny Roads Goldberg, in February of 2014 -- and it definitely looks like the "Sabrina, the Teenage Witch" star has been able to keep it all off!
