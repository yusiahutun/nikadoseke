The 6 Best Eyelash Curlers

April 16, 2024
blog

1

Kevyn Aucoin The Eyelash Curler

1

Kevyn Aucoin The Eyelash Curler

Makeup artist Meredith Baraf, who works with celebs like Lea Michele and Katharine McPhee, is a fan of this eyelash curler’s “good grip” and versatile shape, which works for “most eye shapes.” For the best shape, Baraf recommends holding the curler down for “a good 20 seconds.”

2

Surratt Beauty Relevée Lash Curler

2

Surratt Beauty Relevée Lash Curler

Now 50% Off

When celebrity makeup artist and LORAC Artistry Advisor Kelsey Deenihan is working clients like Reese Witherspoon and Lucy Hale, she trusts this curler. “I’ve tried them all and this is my favorite by far,” says Deenihan. “You truly get a bend in the lashes for instant lift without pinching or fear of lash breakage.”

3

Chella Heated Eyelash Curler

3

Chella Heated Eyelash Curler

Now 15% Off

A heated eyelash curler may sound scary, but don’t worry. This one has “gentle heat that helps lock the curl in place,” according to Renee. “It offers more control and works on even the straightest of lashes, and helps blend false lashes.”

Shiseido Eyelash Curler

Shiseido Eyelash Curler

Renee also loves this “tried and true” tool from Shiseido. “Whether you’re a pro at curling your lashes, or brand new to the game, this curler achieves beautiful lashes in a flash,” she raves. 

5

Tweezerman Champagne Every Lash Curler

5

Tweezerman Champagne Every Lash Curler

Even if you already have a standard curler, you might consider adding this multipurpose tool to your kit. “It’s great for all different eye shapes and makes it easy to curl those hard-to-reach areas,” says  Courtney Summers, makeup artist and founder of the app VaultBeauty. “You can customize your lash curl or even use it to apply fake lashes.”

6

JAPONESQUE Go Curl Eyelash Curler

JAPONESQUE Go Curl Eyelash Curler

For on-the-go touchups, Summers loves this handy curler. “I love compact eyelash curlers you can throw in your purse. This one is easy to use and gives your lashes a beautiful curl,” she says.
