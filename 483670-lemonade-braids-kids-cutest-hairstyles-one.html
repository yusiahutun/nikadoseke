Lemonade braids for kids complement many outfits and suit almost all occasions. They're simple to make and look great on children of all ages and hair types. So, if you have a child who enjoys playing with her hair, these braids would be ideal for her.

What are lemonade braids for kids? They are long, side-swept cornrows that usually fall past the chest or waist. They are low-maintenance, last long, and can be done with natural hair or extensions.
