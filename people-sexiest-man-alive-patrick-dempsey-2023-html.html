Step aside, Chris Evans. Patrick Dempsey has been crowned People’s Sexiest Man Alive, doctor’s orders. The McDreamy Grey’s Anatomy physician has been given the honor, surprisingly, for the first time, potentially taking the honor away from an influencer.
