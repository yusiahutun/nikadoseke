Gabrielle Union shares a kiss with her costar Keith Powers while filming The Perfect Find

October 09, 2024
blog

Gabrielle Union was seen kissing her costar Keith Powers on the set of the upcoming rom-com flick The Perfect Find on Wednesday evening.

The 48-year-old actress and her castmate, 28, appeared to be having a good time as they shared a bottle of champagne while filming on the Brooklyn Bridge.

The forthcoming feature is based on Tia Williams' 2016 novel of the same name and is currently expected to be released on the Netflix streaming service in the near future. 

Getting heavy: Gabrielle Union was seen planting a kiss on her costar Keith Powers while filming a scene for The Perfect Find on the Brooklyn Bridge on Wednesday evening

Union was costumed in a white sleeved dress that draped over much of her shapely form and featured a blue section on her lower back.

The actress complemented her outfit with a pair of platform sneakers that featured various patterned green portions.

Numa Perrier, best known for her work on the 2019 feature Jezebel, was also confirmed to be helming the feature.

The filmmaker also collaborated on the script with Leigh Davenport, who acts as an executive producer on the project. 

In addition to starring in the upcoming flick, Union serves as one of its producers. 

Storyline: The feature will follow a former fashion journalist whose chance for a second shot at success are hampered when she becomes enamored with a coworker who also happens to be her boss' son

The movie will follow a forty-year-old former fashion journalist and designer named Jenna Jones, who receives a second shot at success after she lands a job at a company run by an old rival.

However, the character's efforts to reestablish herself are hampered when she becomes attracted to one of her coworkers, who also happens to be her boss' son. 

Performers such as La La Anthony, Aisha Hinds and Janet Hubert are set to round out the cast of the feature. 

Stacked cast: Other performers such as La La Anthony and Aisha Hinds are set to appear in the upcoming feature 

Niecy Nash was originally supposed to appear in the upcoming film, although she was eventually replaced by Gina Torres. 

Principal photography on the feature began last month and is currently scheduled to run throughout the summer. 

The Perfect Find will be released on the streaming service at an unspecified date in the future.
