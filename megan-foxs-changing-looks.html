Megan Fox's Beauty Evolution From the '00s to Now

May 15, 2024
blog

From her early days playing Makaela Banes in Transformers to her recent Machine Gun Kelly-inspired makeover, Megan Fox has experimented with a number of beauty aesthetics. Whether she's channeling Pam Anderson or coordinating outfits with her beau, Fox continues to serve up some serious beauty and fashion inspiration.

Ahead, we chronicle her beauty evolution from the '00s to today.

2001

Fox spent her teenage years in the early aughts, hence, the pencil-thin brows, glossy lips, and stick-straight hair that define her look here.

2004

Fox went high-gloss for the premiere of Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen in sparkly silver shadow. Her raven retro waves add a touch of Old Hollywood glam.

2004

The bronzed beauty got creative with a cinnamon-hued updo. The shade perfectly complements her skin's warm undertones. Bronzer, anyone?

2005

The Hope & Faith star showed off her freckles with an all-natural look. To get her defined lashes, prep with an eyelash curler before applying lengthening mascara.

2007

Following her star-making turn in Transformers, Fox tried out a retro bouffant for the GQ Men of the Year awards. This might not be an everyday style — unless you're Cher, that is — but if you've got fine tresses it's ace at instantly boosting volume and feigning fullness.

2008

The actress let her natural beauty shine, playing up her best features. A little pink gloss goes a long way in enhancing her Jolie-esque lips at the MTV Movie Awards. So simple, yet so stunning.

2009

Fox chose a low ponytail with a sleek center part for the Berlin premiere of Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen. It's effortless sophistication at its finest.

2009

The Jennifer's Body bombshell made the most of her natural beauty with light gloss and loose waves. Meanwhile, sultry eye makeup highlights her enchanting blue eyes.

2009

How do you get a flawless complexion? Follow Fox's lead and invest in the right makeup and skincare products. Strong brows and dark, loose waves lend a nice contrast.

2011

Fox captured quintessential Hollywood glam at the Golden Globes. Long sculpted waves add movement and dimension to her mane's chocolate brown color. "I gradually lightened her hair," said Zand. "She went from jet black to a medium brown with golden highlights."

2012

The new mom wore her locks long and straight for a gala event. But the real star of the show is her dewy skin.

2013

"Megan wanted to have a moment on the red carpet, showing off a more confident, sophisticated, and softer side," Fox's hairstylist Renato Campora said of her Golden Globes waves. Her subtle smoky eye keeps with the aesthetic.

2014

The face of Avon's Instinct fragrances wore her ombré locks in loose waves. Her light pink makeup lends a romantic undertone.

2015

This Fox-y lady showed off her bold brows and impressive cheekbones with simple light facial makeup and a gleaming fuchsia lip.

2016

The Transformers actress wore an '80s-inspired bubble gum pink lip and silvery eye makeup that has us swooning. And did you notice her brows? Those are some impeccable arches.

2017

At the Liverpool Fashion Fest, Fox donned long ombré waves that feel it-girl cool. If you're the low-maintenance type, this gradual brown-to-blonde blend makes experimenting with color a breeze.

2019

Here, the brunette bombshell pairs messy beach waves with a neutral makeup palette. The result is visually effortless.

2021

After her divorce from Brian Austin Green, Fox started a new chapter of her life that included a new flame, Machine Gun Kelly, and a new look. Long and tousled jet-black hair with deep burgundy lips and bold brows make her a force to be reckoned with.

2022

Fox stepped out with a full-on fringe at the Billboard Music Awards that made us do a double take. Not only do bangs totally transform her look, but their lash-grazing length highlights her heavily-lined eyes. Not ready to commit to bangs? Consider clip-ins.

2022

At "Machine Gun Kelly's Life In Pink," premiere, Fox took a theme and ran with it. From her ultra-long blush pink tresses to her frosted pink makeup and two-toned pink ensemble, she definitely showed up to support her fiancé — and established herself as a beauty icon while doing so.
