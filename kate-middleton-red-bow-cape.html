The Princess of Wales gets festive in a red bow cape

May 07, 2024
blog

The Princess of Wales is getting ready for the festive season, today stepping out in a delightfully Christmassy red bow-festooned cape for her latest royal duties.

Kate – joined by her husband Prince William, as well as in-laws King Charles III and Queen Camilla – attended a special welcome ceremony for President Yoon Suk Yeol and First Lady Kim Keon Hee of South Korea, at London's Horse Guards Parade.

For such a special occasion, the princess welcomed the upcoming festive season in a bright red cape dress by Catherine Walker, featuring a large red bow on its neckline. She added to her statement look by wearing the same scarlet hue head-to-toe; she accessorised with suede Gianvito Rossi heels, a wide-brimmed Jane Taylor hat and a Miu Miu clutch bag.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

Beauty-wise, the Princess of Wales opted for an elegant chignon, which worked perfectly with the shape of her hat. Instead of her usual subtle make-up look, Kate opted for a dark brown smoky eye and berry lip, perfect for this time of year.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Royal fans will recognise her special jewellery choice, as Kate finished her look with two items previously belonging to Princess Diana: her famous sapphire and diamond engagement ring (which was voted the most-wanted celebrity ring back in 2020) and matching sapphire and diamond drop earrings. William also got into the festive spirit, by matching his wife's ensemble with a red tie.
