Ryan Reynolds record Wrexham signing KOd by massive error after Deadline Day drama

May 28, 2024
blog

Ryan Reynolds’ record Wrexham signing has fallen through thanks to what fans are describing as a major “c*** up”.

The Red Dragons appeared to have pulled off a late Deadline Day swoop by signing Harrogate Town star Luke Armstrong. Wrexham had looked unlikely to land their man with Friday’s deadline fast approaching.

Sulphurites boss Simon Weaver claimed a “significant” fee, reportedly in the region of £500,000, would have to be forked out for his striker. Yet Wrexham managed to tempt the North Yorkshire side into seemingly parting ways with the 27-year-old late in the day, with their bid exceeding the £300,000 they splurged on Ollie Palmer in January 2022.

However Wrexham confirmed on Saturday morning they were not able to get the deal over the line.
