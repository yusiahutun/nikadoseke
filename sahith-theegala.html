Professional Career

With the start of the 2020 season, Theegala won the title of Ben Hogan Award, Haskins Award, and the Jack Nicklaus Award and became the fifth personality to achieve all three titles in the same year. Theegala began his professional journey in June 2020 at the age of 23 at the Outlaw Tour’s Lone Tree Classic where he achieved third place.

He finished his match on T-14 at the 2020 Safeway Open on the PGA Tour. Later, he participated in T-19 at the Korn Ferry Tour event and the 2021 Chitimacha Louisiana Open. He also participated in the T-9 MGM Resorts Championship at Paiute and finished T-4 at the Nationwide Children’s Hospital Championship and thereby competed while being 6th at the Korn Ferry Tour Championship.

He made consecutive top 10 finishes in the Korn Ferry Tour Finals and secured a place for himself in the PGA Tour Card for the season 2021-22. Later, he attained his top-10 finish on the PGA Tour in October 2021 at Sanderson Farms Championship. During Saturday’s third round at the PGA Tour’s Fortinet Championship in California, Theegala skillfully orchestrated a series of birdies on the back-nine, catapulting himself into a commanding two-stroke lead.

His impressive performance included a stellar five-under-par 67, positioning him distinctly ahead of the competition as they approach the decisive final round on Sunday. This marks Theegala’s promising pursuit of his inaugural individual title on the prestigious tour.
