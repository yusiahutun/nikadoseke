Love Island bombshell Antigoni Buxton has revealed the ins and outs of the show's secret "shower politics".

The blonde singer, 26, chatted exclusively to the Daily Star about some of the show's biggest secrets and her exciting new music.

When asked how she found the cleanliness of the villa, after her co-star Jay Younger revealed how "grim" and "vile" it was to live there - Antigoni said it got the green light.

"The girls had a little shower situation where we had to shotgun shower first, shotgun shower second, and all that but I used to shower outside because a lot of the time I would wash my hair in the morning or whatever so I wasn't really too involved in the politics of the shower, I was just like cool."

When asked if she wanted to replicate a sexy shower scene like I'm A Celeb's Mylene Klass, the Islander laughed and said: "I mean, I definitely wasn't looking sexy, it was just very practical to have a shower before we had to look glamorous in the garden."

The singer-songwriter also announced some news of her own as her brand new single You Can Have Him drops on Friday (September 2).

Talking about her new song, she revealed: "It's the idea of if you can take my man, then you can have him, it's kind of like a girl power vibe.

"It's not saying I don't care, it's saying I want to be with someone who loves me so much that no one can sway him."

"I'm excited for it to come out, we have a simple visualiser for it but we are planning a music video for it at the moment."

But will there be any appearances from her stunning ITV co-stars?

She added: "Do you know what, we've thought about it but we will have to see what happens. It depends where the stories go, but I would love the girls to come through and have Tasha and Danica dancing in it.

"There will be some dancing from me as well as the song will definitely lend itself to some good choreography... I want to fuse my Cypriot heritage with the UK hip-hop sound I've grown up around so it's about bringing those two worlds together."
