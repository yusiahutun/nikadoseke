Natalie Portmans Best Red Carpet Looks

January 28, 2024
blog

Natalie Portman has been a Hollywood mainstay since she was 11 years old, and after three decades in the business, the Oscar winner, Marvel superhero, and soccer club founder has also embraced her role as a red carpet powerhouse.

The long-time face of Dior has seen a style evolution that's taken her from sweet gowns and streamlined Jackie O-inspired looks to micro-minis, short shorts, and midriffs. Though Portman has embraced a more whimsical take on fashion of late, she's maintained her keen eye for couture detailing and eco-friendly styles, frequently pulling archival looks and unique vintage pieces.

From jewel-toned sets to ballerina gowns, we're breaking down Portman's top red carpet looks over the years.

2023: In Dior Haute Couture at Paris Fashion Week

Long a fan of strapless styles, Portman attended the Dior Haute Couture show in a sweet floral dress from the fashion house, adorned with a petite Miss Dior bag.

2023: In a Matching Dior Set at the 'May/December' Premiere

Portman got rave reviews at the Cannes Film Festival for her film May/December, but also for her brick red Dior twinset featuring a belted mini dress and casually draped blazer.

2023: In a Tiered Dior Gown at the Cannes Film Festival

Portman dazzled the evening red carpet at Cannes in a reimagining of Dior's 1949 Junon gown, which echoed the actress's Oscar-winning turn as a ballerina in Black Swan.

2023: In a Miu Miu Shorts Set at the 'Angel City' Premiere

Portman perfectly combined glamour and sport in a bouclé short suit by Miu Miu at the premiere of Angel City, a documentary about the founding of her all-female-owned Los Angeles soccer club.

2022: In Vintage at the 'Thor: Love and Thunder' Premiere

Officially in her mini era, Portman sported a vintage Claude Montana suit to the Rome photocall of Thor: Love and Thunder.

2022: In a Sparkling Céline Couture Minidress in L.A.

Portman continued to embrace short hemlines at the LA premiere of Thor: Love and Thunder, but this time swapped color for sparkles in a dazzling Céline Couture dress, Jimmy Choo heels, and Anita Ko Jewelry.

2020: In a Secret Message Dior Gown at the Oscars

A vocal supporter of women's rights, Portman accessorized her Dior gown at the 92nd Academy Awards with a matching cape featuring the names of female directors she believed were snubbed from that year's Best Director's race.

2019: In Dior Haute Couture at the Vanity Fair Afterparty

Two of Portman's favorite elements—a sweetheart neckline and tapered trousers—met in the Dior Haute Couture jumpsuit she wore to the 2019 Vanity Fair Oscar after-party.

2019: In Dior Separates at the Variety Power of Women Event

This time, tapered trousers and fitted sweaters found themselves on the red carpet when Portman channeled Jackie O. in a streamlined look from Dior for Variety's Power of Women event.

2019: In Deep-V Givenchy at the 'Lucy in the Sky' Premiere

Portman went for an LBD with a twist at the Lucy in the Sky premiere in LA, walking the red carpet in a long-sleeved black Givenchy mini-dress with a plunging neckline and minimal jewels.

2018: In Head-to-Toe Gucci Sparkles in Venice

Portman lit up the red carpet at the 75th Venice Film Festival in a metallic custom Gucci gown featuring exaggerated shoulders, long sleeves, and a just-sweet-enough cut-out and black bow.

2017: In Streamlined Yellow Prada for the Golden Globes

Portman's former stylist, Kate Young, wanted to make sure the actress (who was pregnant at the time) had something exciting but comfortable to wear for the 2017 Golden Globe Awards and landed on an embellished Prada gown. "It had that 1960s feel, which was reminiscent of her film, Jackie, but the yellow color made it modern and unexpected.”

2015: In a Lacey Pink Lanvin Gown in Toronto

For the premiere of A Tale of Love and Darkness at the Toronto International Film Festival, Portman wore a custom Lanvin lace dress with a vintage-inspired cut, in one of her favorite shades: ballet slipper pink.

2013: In Dior Haute Couture in Paris

Not one to shy away from separates on the red carpet, Portman paired a corset-like embellished top and trousers from Dior Haute Couture with classic black pumps for the Esprit Dior, Miss Dior exhibition in Paris.

2013: In a Metallic Dior Maxi in London

Pairing it with a simple black cashmere sweater, Portman let a silver jacquard Dior maxi skirt shine at the Thor: The Dark World premiere in London.

2012: In Archival Dior at the Academy Awards

According to Young, Portman's vintage polka dot dress for the 84th Academy Awards was meant to be. “For the Oscars, we were looking at the Dior archives and found this dress from 1954. It fit her perfectly with not even one alteration."

2012: In Custom Lanvin at the Golden Globes

Alber Elbaz, Lanvin's designer at the time, made six sketches for Natalie to choose from for the 2012 Golden Globe Awards. Ultimately, Young says, "We really liked the weird color combination and the fact that the silhouette had a little bit of drama. The kick on the side made it look cool and architectural.”

2012: In Giambattista Valli Haute Couture at the SAG Awards

Although she's come to embrace a range of bright, warm tones, according to Young, Portman initially was drawn to pinks, purples, and burgundy, like this gown from Giambattista Valli that she wore to the SAG Awards.

2011: In a White Azzaro Gown at the SAG Awards

A pregnant Portman wore an iridescent white Azzaro gown to the SAG Awards in 2011, where she won as Lead Actress for her role in Black Swan.

2006: In Chanel Haute Couture at the Golden Globes

Young says that they landed on this black lace Chanel Haute Couture dress with a sweetheart neckline for the 2006 Golden Globes after scouring Paris for the perfect look. "She had short hair at the time and it looked adorable on her. It just felt right.”
