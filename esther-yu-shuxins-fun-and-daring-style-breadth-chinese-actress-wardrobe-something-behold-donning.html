Esther Yu Shuxins fun and daring style: the breadth of the Chinese actress wardrobe is somethi

October 09, 2024
blog

In addition to her acting talent, she’s become something of a fashion icon, and was appointed as a brand ambassador for Moschino in July.

Here’s a peek inside her wardrobe and her best styles to date:

1. Pretty in pink

Yu has embraced Barbiecore of late through a series of all-pink ensembles. In one photo posted to Instagram, she looks quirky and cute in an all-pink skater skirt and matching top, and in another, effortlessly pulls off a Marine Serre jacket of the same hue.

2. Floral delight

Yu also embraces floral patterns in her outfits. She’s been seen wearing a beautiful floral crop top from Paco Rabanne, paired with a classic Gucci white horsebit tote bag. And during her holidays, she continues to incorporate florals into her wardrobe, opting for charming flower dresses, as seen in another pic posted by the star.

3. Playing with colours

Yu experiments with different colour tones, often opting for monochromatic white and black outfits. On one occasion, she chose a black plaid dress, complemented by black block heels and a matching leather bag. In another post, she combined a cream-coloured corset and baggy white sweatpants, along with white trainers for a brunch outing.

On another occasion she paired a purple jacket with floral jeans, and rocked a cute outdoorsy look when she donned a striped tank top, denim overalls and a blue handbag from Chrome Hearts.

4. Showstopping dresses

Yu isn’t afraid to make a grand entrance, as demonstrated by her appearance at a Givenchy event, where she shone in a white lace dress and white boots.

At the Weibo Awards 2023, she turned heads again in a stunning, hand-embroidered halter neck gown from the Rahul Mishra couture’s spring 2022 collection, pairing the dazzling piece with equally dazzling Tiffany & Co. jewellery.

5. Dopamine dressing

Yu enjoys rocking playful and bold looks too. In one street photo shoot she can be seen wearing a gathered wrap minidress from Alex Perry, which she pairs with Givenchy’s shiny pink “Shark Lock” boots and a bedazzled Hello Kitty bag. In another post, she opts for a bright green “Fanta” T-shirt paired with orange jeans.

6. Balletcore

Drawing inspiration from Blackpink’s Jennie – who is known for her love of balletcore fashion – Yu has been known to incorporate ballet influences into her wardrobe too. In one photo she combines a black tank top with a pink tutu, and finishes the look with ballet flats from Moschino, a sequin mirror bag, and pink ribbons in her hair.

7. Vibrant rainbow

And who says colour blocking is the only way to go? Yu’s typically bold approach means she’s embraced vibrant mixed colour ensembles on many occasions too. In one post she is seen showing off all the colours of the rainbow in a playful Alémais dress. In another, she serves big time holiday vibes in a colourful crochet Moschino dress adorned with various shapes and icons.

From daring silhouettes to playful hues, Yu continues to surprise and delight fans with her sartorial choices – and we’re all for it.
