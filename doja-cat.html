Who is Doja Cat?

Doja Cat, also known as Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer.

She is popular for her enthralling music infused with electronic R&B and hip-hop influences.

Interested in pursuing a career in music from a young age, she released her debut EP ‘Purrr!’ at the age of 18.

Doja Cat Career 

Doja Cat released her first-ever single ‘So High’ in the year 2013. The following year, she came to prominence after signing to RCA Records and releasing her debut EP, ‘Purrr!’

After this, she released the single ‘No Police.’ She then began launching her music on YouTube and Soundcloud.

Doja also started collaborating with other artists, including Hellboy, Elliphant, Pregnant Boy, and Skoolie Escobar.

On July 13, 2017, she got featured on L8LOOMER’s track ‘Right Side.’ On February 1, 2018, the singer came up with her single “Roll with Us”.

The following month, she released the second track from her upcoming album called ‘Go to Town’ along with its music video. Doja released her full-length debut album ‘Amala’ on March 30, 2018.

On August 10, 2018, she released the song ‘Mooo!’ on her YouTube channel. The track quickly went viral. Its music video went on to garner attention from several well-known artists, including Chris Brown and Chance the Rapper.

The video has received millions of views to date. On August 31, 2018, Doja Cat came up with a single version of the viral video “Mooo!”.

The singer has a massive fan following on social media. She has around 800k followers on Instagram and almost half a million subscribers on YouTube.

Today, Doja has emerged as one of the most popular upcoming artists in the USA. Blessed with an incredible voice and amazing musical skills, she is well on her way to build a reputation for herself in the highly competitive music world.

The singer, who is inspired by Pharrell, Drake, PartyNextDoor, and Erykah Badu, herself serves as an inspiration for many young professionals in the industry.
