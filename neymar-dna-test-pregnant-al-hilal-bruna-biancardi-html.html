Neymar 'demands DNA test to confirm four-month pregnant Brazilian lover is expecting his third child

July 10, 2024
blog

Neymar has reportedly demanded a DNA test after claims he is set to father a third child with a third different woman.

Reports emerged in Brazilian outlet Portal LeoDias that Neymar had an affair with Brazilian model and influencer Amanda Kimberlly while he was still with his former partner Bruna Biancardi.

The report claims Kimberlly is now four months pregnant, with news of the pregnancy coming as a surprise to Neymar.


Despite the surprise, Neymar was said to 'receive the news with joy', and wants to carry out a DNA test as soon as the child is born to confirm it is his.

Kimberlly hails from Sao Paulo and is best known for appearing on Brazilian dating show 'Are you the one?' in 2016, with the model getting to know the Al-Hilal star soon after.

Since then, Kimberlly - who will reportedly give birth to a girl - has dated several celebrities and currently works for aid organisation Project Gaia. 

Portal LeoDias reported further information about the pregnancy earlier this month.

In a report, the outlet said: 'Neymar is going to be a father for the third time. The mother is a Brazilian model who is three months pregnant.

'She fell pregnant while they were having an affair, including while the footballer was recovering from a knee injury.

'There is concern about the health of the baby's mother, who recently had to undergo some treatments. The pregnancy was a surprise, but they were having a romance.'

News of the pregnancy also comes after Neymar only became a father for the second time just three months ago in October when daughter Mavie was born.

He then split with the mother of Mavie, Bruna Biancardi in November, amid rumours of the Brazilian star being unfaithful.

Neymar had faced accusations of cheating on Biancardi with compromising footage emerging last year of him partying with two different women in a Spanish nightclub. 

In fact, a report in BILD even claimed the pair had a bizarre sex contract that allowed Neymar to flirt and sleep with other women.

Even more strangely, there were specific conditions for the contract. This included that the Brazilian was not allow to kiss women on the mouth, had to use a condom during sex and that the relationship must stay secret. 

There are also allegations he asked Brazilian OnlyFans star Aline Farias for nudes, with Farias reportedly releasing screenshots of her conversation with Neymar. 

Meanwhile, in June he was dogged by further allegations of infidelity with influencer Fernanda Campos, prompting him to post a groveling apology to Biancardi on social media.

Neymar - who also has a son from a previous relationship with Brazilian influencer Carolina Dantas - was with Biancardi since 2021.

They kept their romance private before going Instagram official in January 2022. However, they announced the end of the relationship in August 2022, before rekindling it soon after. 

Posting on her Instagram story in November, Biancardi then confirmed the news of their split as she said: 'This is a private matter, but since I am often associated with news, suspicions and jokes, I would like to inform you that I am not in a relationship.

'We are Mavie’s parents and that is the reason for our relationship. I hope you won't associate me with the news so often anymore. Thank you.'

The latest news in Neymar's private life comes amid a difficult time on the pitch. The 31-year-old completed a £78m move to the Saudi Arabian side Al-Hilal in August from PSG, signing a two-year deal worth £130m-per-season.

However, in October he suffered a serious knee injury while playing for Brazil in their 2-0 defeat to Uruguay in World Cup qualifying.

Neymar is set to miss the rest of the campaign after rupturing the anterior cruciate ligament and meniscus in his left knee.

Neymar was reportedly removed from Al-Hilal's squad for the rest of the season as boss Jorge Jesus looked to create room for a new signing from abroad.
