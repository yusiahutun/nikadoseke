Where was Burnie Burns born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Michael Justin “Burnie” Burns entered the world on January 18, 1973, in Rochester, New York. At Alief Elsik High School, he was known as “Burnie,” and afterward he attended the University of Texas at Austin, where he got a bachelor’s degree in computer science. K29HW-D is a student television station in Texas, and Burns volunteered there.

How did Burnie Burns start his Professional Career?

In 1997, Burnie Burns and his housemates filmed the movie The Schedule. He collaborated on the direction, production, and script for the picture.
