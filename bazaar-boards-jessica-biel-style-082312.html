Jessica Biel Style - Jessica Biel Red Carpet Style

February 29, 2024
blog

Media Platforms Design Team

Whether on the red carpet promoting her new flick Total Recall, or working it during off duty mode, Jessica Biel is killing it in the style sphere as of late. The actress has been keeping it on the lighter side of the spectrum — perhaps in preparation for a certain white gown in her near future — including a jumpsuit by Valentino (bottom left), a feathered creation from Giambattista Valli Couture (middle) and a demure chantilly dress by her rumored bridal designer, Elie Saab (bottom right). Other star looks explored her pastel side in a stunning strapless pink dress by Raf Simons's debut collection for Dior Couture (second from top left), a '50s-inspired yellow frock by Zac Posen (top right) and an unexpected, but chic belly baring number by Christian Cota (second from bottom left). But our favorite just might be the sportif white Chanel minidress (top left) she paired boldly with green sandals. Seven stellar looks in nearly as many days? Bravo, Ms. Biel.

Kerry Pieri is Harper's Bazaar's Digital Fashion/Features Director. Kerry oversees the fashion vertical for BAZAAR.com, including launching digital covers, styling celebrity features, and managing recurring features including Brand Watch and My Life in Three Looks. In addition, Kerry edits the “best of” each season, from shoes to bags, denim and coats, and is consistently looking for shifts in the fashion realm to lend a voice. Kerry formerly served as the Editorial Director of  StyleCaster.com and as a producer at Full Frontal Fashion. She lives in New York with her husband Steve, and baby girl Lila Sky.
