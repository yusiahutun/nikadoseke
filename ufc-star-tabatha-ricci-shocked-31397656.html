UFC star Tabatha Ricci has revealed an embarrassing encounter she had while she was on the date.

Last in action in June when she defeated Gillian Robertson via unanimous decision, 'Baby Shark' is scheduled to take on Loopy Godinez at UFC 295 this Saturday (November 11).

A win against her Mexican opponent will see her among the title contenders at 125lbs next year. While the Brazilian is a mixed martial artist through-and-through, she's also got a life outside of the cage and opened up about a horror dating experience she had during an appearance on the ‘S** and Violence with Rebel Girl’ podcast which is hosted by fellow UFC fighter Ashlee Evans-Smith.
